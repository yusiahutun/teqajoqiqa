Naivasha: 14 People Killed as Pwani University Bus Collides With Matatu

Fourteen people, including Pwani University students and their lecturers, have died after the school bus they were travelling in was involved in a grisly road accident.

In the Thursday, March 30 mishap, the bus collided with a 14-seater matatu in the Kayole area along the Nairobi-Naivasha Highway before overturning.

The students, reportedly over 30, and their tutors were heading to the University of Eldoret to participate in a sports competition before their journey was cut short.
