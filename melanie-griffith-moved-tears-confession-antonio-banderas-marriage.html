Melanie Griffith left 'moved to tears' with rare confession about marriage to Antonio Banderas

April 02, 2024
blog

Melanie Griffith and Antonio Banderas may have gotten divorced in 2015 after nearly two decades of marriage, but they still look back on their time together fondly.

Such was the case with the actress' latest social media post, going down memory lane as she remembered their life together during Holy Week in Spain.

Semana Santa or Holy Week is an annual tribute of the passion of Christ celebrated by Catholics in many Spanish communities featuring beautiful processions as a form of penance.

Melanie recalled how important of an occasion it was during her marriage from 1996 to 2015 to the Spanish Oscar-nominee, sharing pictures of the celebration that falls between April 2 and 9 this year.

She wrote alongside them: "During the 19 years I was married to Antonio one of the many extraordinary experiences he gave to me and the kids was our introduction to and subsequent love for Semana Santa. Holy Week. 

"It is a very old tradition, that is so very beautiful. During Holy Week over a hundred thrones representing Christ and the Virgin Mary are carried through the streets of Malaga. Every day and night from Palm Sunday to Easter Sunday. It is a magical and mystical week. 

Melanie recalled memories of Semana Santa with ex Antonio

The 65-year-old continued: "Many people follow behind these thrones on their sometimes eight hour long journey. When I first saw Cautivo I was moved to tears. I think of it as a spiritual experience." 

Sharing her love for the religious procession, she detailed her own memories of the occasion, penning: "So for many many years I was privileged to be one of the 30,000 people who follow behind him on his journey through the city.
