Meet Abigail Davies World Darts Championship interviewer who also works for Soccer Saturday

August 20, 2024
blog

Abigail Davies is a familiar face on the darts circuit, and she linked up with Sky Sports to cover the Paddy Power PDC World Darts Championship.

Last winter, Davies covered the event at Ally Pally for talkSPORT, but the freelancer switched to Sky Sports for this season's championship. Abigail, who has also worked for the BBC and ITV, shared at the start of the tournament: "An absolute dream to be part of the Sky Sports Darts team!"

She then expanded on X, formerly known as Twitter: "Six years ago I did my first shift at Sky, as a runner at Ally Pally. A lot of tea making and a MSc degree later, it's a dream to be back at the Worlds for @SkySportsDarts, working every day as a reporter!"

Abigail was name checked by Jeff Stelling when the longstanding Soccer Saturday host stepped down from his duties last May. She told the Daily Mirror: "Jeff Stelling is my ultimate idol.

"When I joined Twitter my bio read 'when I grow up I want to be Jeff Stelling!' Shamefully, he was my first profile picture on Facebook!"

Abigail suffered from an eating disorder when she grew up - and she's now working tirelessly as a mental health ambassador and advocate.
