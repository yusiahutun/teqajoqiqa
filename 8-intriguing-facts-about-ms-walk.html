FAQs

1. What is the purpose of the MS Walk?

The purpose of the MS Walk is to raise funds and awareness for Multiple Sclerosis, a chronic autoimmune disease that affects the central nervous system.

2. Who can participate in the MS Walk?

Anyone can participate in the MS Walk! Whether you are an individual, a family, a team, or a company, you can register and join the walk to support the cause.

3. How long is the MS Walk?

The length of the MS Walk varies from event to event. Some walks are 1 mile, while others can be as long as 5 or more miles. Check with your local MS Society for specific details about the walk in your area.

4. Do I need to fundraise to participate in the MS Walk?

While fundraising is encouraged, it is not mandatory to participate in the MS Walk. However, fundraising helps raise more funds for research, advocacy, and programs that benefit people living with MS.

5. Can I volunteer for the MS Walk if I don’t want to walk?

Absolutely! The MS Walk relies on volunteers to ensure a successful event. Contact your local MS Society to inquire about volunteer opportunities.

6. Can I bring my pet to the MS Walk?

Pets are usually welcome at the MS Walk, but it is best to check with the event organizers beforehand to confirm any specific rules or restrictions regarding pets.

7. Can I participate in multiple MS Walk events?

Yes, you can participate in as many MS Walk events as you like! Each event helps raise funds and awareness for the cause, so the more walks you join, the greater impact you can make.

8. How can I stay informed about upcoming MS Walk events?

You can stay informed about upcoming MS Walk events by visiting the official website of the National MS Society or contacting your local MS Society. They will have the most up-to-date information on upcoming walks in your area.
