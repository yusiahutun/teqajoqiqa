8 best wine advent calendars to toast Christmas this year

August 14, 2024
blog

Whether red, white or rosé (or all three), a wine advent calendar is a great way to celebrate

    A boozy Christmas countdown is one of the most popular advent calendars, and we have to say that wine might just be our favourite.

    Whether it's a Christmas-themed wine rack filled with mini bottles of wine, prosecco or champagne, or a festive box dedicated to just your fave (red, white or rosé all day, anyone?) these elegant wine advent calendars are a fabulous way to discover new wines or enjoy an array of your favourites.

    It's never too early to start thinking about securing a wine advent calendar - last year, the format proved so popular that several brands sold out completely! So get scrolling for our pick of the best...

    How we chose the best wine advent calendars

    • Variety: Whether you love white, red, rosé or prosecco (or a combination) - we've found something for everyone.
    • Popularity: There's a reason that most of the advent calendars included in this edit sold out last year - there are some incredible deals on quality wines that work out far lower than their RRP.
    • Sustainability: Many of the wine advent calendars we've included have reusable packaging that you can fill yourself for years to come.

    Shop the best wine advent calendars for Christmas 2023

    We’ve created an edit of our favourite wine advent calendars from John Lewis, Laithwaites, Virgin Wines and more. Check back for updates as we'll be adding to this as more become available. Here's to Christmas!
