Alexis Floyd Is Playing Neff Davis On Inventing Anna: Who Is She?

Floyd was very fascinated by the power dynamic when she booked the role in the Netflix miniseries two and a half years ago. She had an unconventional audition for Inventing Anna and even met the real-life Neffatari "Neff" Davis while studying to play the part.
