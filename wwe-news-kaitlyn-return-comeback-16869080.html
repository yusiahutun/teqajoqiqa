WWE star Kaitlyn confirms shock RETURN to ring for first time FOUR YEARS

February 24, 2024
blog

The 31-year-old – real name Celeste Bonin – will rejoin the WWE after retiring from the ring in 2014.

She was one of the forerunners of the so-called “Women’s Revolution” which saw female talent given the same status as the men.

Kaitlyn will return to the WWE ring in August competing in the all-women tournament the Mae Young Classic.

The one-time Diva’s Champion had a warm-up match on the indie circuit after spending six months getting back into ring shape.

She has been showing fans were skills were workout and training snaps on Instagram.

Kaitlyn told ESPN: “The thing that got me through [as a rookie in WWE] and the thing that allowed me to achieve my level of success was my attitude, and looking back now, I am coming back with experience.

“I am certainly nervous because the level of competition is so much higher and there are so many different women from around the world and they bring skill levels.

“But it's just so exciting."

Women’s wrestling has changed a lot since she retired, and the star said she was looking to get back into the mix.

She added: “Since I came back, I had the opportunity to work backward and really nail down the fundamentals and the basics – things that I really lacked during my first run with the WWE.

“Now I am bringing these newly honed and developed skills that I have acquired recently and I am combining that with my fearless attitude.

“And because I don't have this rookie vibe about me, now I can really concentrate on surprising the WWE universe with all the things I have learned."

Kaitlyn is a former bodybuilder, and competed in the Arnold Classic competition.

Since being away, the star has turned vegan and claims she feels fitter than ever.

She has also been candid with her battles with addiction and struggles as she divorced her husband PJ Braun.

The star has been working on a new wrestling persona, and sports a new look with long black hair and a full sleeve tattoo.

WWE now has two women’s championships – one for Raw and one for SmackDown Live – and the division is filled with megastars like Ronda Rousey and Charlotte.

Kaitlyn said: “[Wrestling has] seen so many different types of women of all different shapes and sizes with different skills and techniques that it is so exciting to be a part of that world again."

She made her debut with WWE in 2010 when she won the all-women third season of NXT.

The Texas-native won the Divas title in 2013 and had a celebrated feud with AJ Lee.

Since being away, she has become a fitness icon, has her own clothing line, and even opened a smoothie bar.
