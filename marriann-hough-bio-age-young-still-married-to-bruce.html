Meet Marriann Hough, Derek & Julianne Hough’s Mother

Derek and Julianne Hough who garnered fame from Dancing With the Stars are the children of Marriann Hough. Derek and Julianne and their other three siblings, Sharee, Marabeth, and Katherine Hough grew up in Utah.

During their teenage years, amid their parents’ divorce, Derek and Julianne were sent to live in London with dance instructors Corky and Shirley Ballas. Corky and Shirley are the parents of fellow DWTS pro-Mark Ballas, who was in Derek’s wedding party and is still close to the Houghs. Marriann and Bruce pledged to stay committed to their four young children in their formal divorce order, which was written in March 1998, when Julianne was just 9 years old and Derek was 12 years old.

“The parties both have loving and valuable relationships with their children, and agree to work together to support those relationships,” Utah court documents state. “Further, the parties agree to work together to provide for the physical care and financial and emotional support of the children.”

The marriage reportedly ended in 1998.

What Is Marriann Hough Maiden Name?

Marriann Hough’s maiden name is Marriann Heaton. She was reportedly born to her parents Don and Beth Heaton.

Marriann’s parents married on 28 August 1950. They moved to Idaho Falls, where their son Donnie was born on May 10, 1952. Following their relocation to Pocatello, Idaho, on February 20, 1955, their second son, Bob, was born. After that, they relocated back to Idaho Falls, where their son Carey was born on September 25, 1959, and their daughter Marriann was born on February 23, 1957.

Don put in a lot of effort and worked many jobs to support his family.
