7 Fantastic Ted Lasso Costumes

July 08, 2024
blog

Ted Lasso came to its heartfelt end this year with its third and final season. While we're holding out hope that the Richmond team will be back in some form, we're going to keep our love for the Apple TV+ series going with a Ted Lasso-inspired Halloween costume in 2023. Ted Lasso characters Keeley Jones, Roy Kent, Rebecca Welton, and Ted Lasso are the perfect characters to dress up as this Halloween or any occasion that requires a costume.

Whether you're looking for a group costume, couples costume, or solo costume, Ted Lasso provides ample inspiration and opportunities to create your best costume yet. Gather your friends and dress up as the Richmond faithful for a show-stopping group Halloween costume, or pair up as Keeley and Roy (or Keeley and Jamie!) for the best couples costume. We've done all the work for you and found the perfect items to create your DIY costume. With these ideas you're sure to win Halloween this year! (P.S. Shop your closet first. You can get everything else you need on Amazon.)
