Davina McCall 'always wears' sexy lingerie as she admits she loves stripping off

The Masked Singer judge Davina McCall has admitted she likes to strut around in provocative lingerie even when she's home alone as it makes her feel sexy.

Davina, 54, admitted that she gets pleasure from the feeling being in her undies gives her so she strips to wearing them wherever possible.

Talking about her love of strolling around wearing next to nothing she said: “Underwear makes me feel sexy. Sexy lingerie. Nicely coloured lingerie.

“I always wear lovely lingerie, whether I’m alone or in company. It always makes me feel great.”

But the fitness nut said she wouldn’t be so open about her romance with hairdresser Michael Douglas, 47, admitting they have an agreement to keep their love life private.

She told OK! Magazine: “We’ve got an agreement that I don’t talk about our relationship, because our exes and kids don’t have a right to reply, so it’s not fair.

"We won’t ever talk about us in an interview because somehow words linger and we don’t want that. The podcast is pretty public but our relationship isn’t, and that will never change because we don’t need it to."

Davina also talked about her role on The Masked Singer as she praised the show for being "fun."

She said: "I am so excited. It’s so much fun. We never stop laughing. The celebs’ costumes are amazing this season but my guessing is pretty rubbish this time around."

Davina also confessed to thinking that fellow judge Jonathan Ross has the best changing room but they aren't allowed to mix backstage due to the risk of spotting any of the celebrity contestants out of their disguise.

And she admitted that despite knowing some of the contestants on a personal level the judges still didn't manage to guess their identities.

Speaking to MailOnline, Davina said: "What might be a massive name for Mo might not be a massive name for me and vice versa.

"There's a big name for me, there's a big name for Mo. He couldn't believe it. There was someone I know, that was quite weird."

Host Joel Dommett added: "Someone Rita knows quite personally."

When asked if Rita guessed her friend, Joel replied: "No, absolutely not, it was a huge shock. It was brilliant."
