Jennice Ontiveros From Below Deck Is The First Female Deckhand

July 30, 2024
blog

Jennice Ontiveros is known to appear in season two of Bravo TV’s Below Deck. During the show, she got caught up in the drama that involved romance, but apart from the drama, she caught the attention of the viewers with her kind and hardworking nature.

She had been intrigued by water and beaches since she was young, and she fulfilled her interest when she landed a deck position. It is said that she has backed out of this industry. She has also worked on the documentary series American Greed.

She had made an impact on the show as she was the only female on the deck, which didn’t hold her back. In the show, she is also seen juggling between her work ethic and her personal life.

Her time on the show was limited to one season because she decided not to return. 

Jennice Ontiveros Celebrates Her Birthday On June 1

Jennice Ontiveros’s age is around 33 years old. According to the bio provided by Bravo TV, she was 25 years old when she joined season 2 of Below Deck.

She celebrates her birthday on June 1 with her close friends and family.

She was born and raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico. She discovered her love for water when she was at Elephant Butte Lake, and to be closer to the beach and Hollywood, she moved to Los Angeles when she was 18 years old.

She graduated from Whittier College with a bachelor’s degree in business administration, theater, and communications arts. When she attended college, she was president of the Thespian Society and the Hispanic Student Association. She was a recipient of the Keck Foundation Fellowship Program.

After her graduation, she moved to Chicago, where she landed a job in documentary television. In the summer of 2013, she got her first deck position on Lake Michigan.

She Left Yachting And Joined Entertainment Industry

Below Deck star, Jennice Ontiveros appeared on the show for season 2, but she didn’t return for the next season. According to Screenrant, she has left yachting and joined the entertainment industry.

She is a narrator for the documentary series Cosmos: Possible Worlds, which can be streamed on Fox and Hulu. She also worked as a producer on the Netflix series Schoolof Chocolate and on the YouTube Original series Create Together. She also served as a narrator in the series Create Together.

According to her Instagram, she loves to travel to new places. She has 7,490 followers on the platform under the profile @jenniceonearth.

She Is The First Female Deckhand On The Season 2 Of Below Deck

Ontiveros became the first female deckhand on Below Deck season 2. She shared her struggles about being the only female on the deck, but she paved the way for future female deckhands on the show.

By being the only female on the deck, she felt like an outcast because of her height, and she had a hard time carrying heavy objects. During season 2 of the show, she was a hardworking and kind deckhand.

During her interview with Pop City Life, she mentioned that she didn’t let anyone discourage her; instead, she practiced hard.

While she was on the show, she became involved in a lot of drama, and she and a fellow crew member sparked a romance while they were on the deck. 

Jennice And Fellow Deckhand Kelley Johnson Had A Fling

On the show, Jennice and her crewmate Kelley Johnson began dating. Ontiveros had tried not to involve herself in a relationship with Kelley as she had a boyfriend at the time.

She had turned down her crewmate’s romantic proposal many times, but she couldn’t hide her feelings toward him for a long time, so she decided to take their relationship from crewmate to lover.

The couple became inseparable throughout the charter season, but when the reunion came around, they were not on speaking terms and had a bad breakup. Ontiveros wanted her lover to move in with her when they returned, but Johnson was not ready for such a big commitment.

It is known that Johnson allegedly wrote negative comments about Jennice online and didn’t treat her with respect while they were in a romantic relationship.

During the interview with The Daily Dish, Kelley revealed that the way he ended his relationship with Jennice is one of his biggest regrets and that he wishes that his relationship with Ontiveros ended differently.
