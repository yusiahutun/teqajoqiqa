Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck share steamy 'Valentine's Kiss' at singer's movie premiere

August 02, 2024
blog

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck have been spotted sharing a ‘Valentine’s kiss’ on the Red Carpet as they attend the premiere of her new Amazon original This Is Me…Now: A Love Story.

The celebrity couple looked as cute as ever, as they co-ordinated in black on the Red Carpet. They’re always there to support one another for their achievements, and their latest appearance comes shortly after Jennifer Lopez opened up about why their first relationship ‘crumbled down.’

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck pack on the PDA in lastest public appearance

On the carpet, which was filled with rose petals, the duo looked dapper in all-black as they were all smiles arm in arm.

Ben Affleck opted for a simple black tux, but the Jenny from the Block singer went for anything but simple. She stunned in a low-cut strapless black gown, showing off her curves. But it was her skirt that was grabbing all the attention.

The black maxi skirt, with shimmering silver detail, looks pretty standard at first glance, but upon closer glance, at the bottom, the piece features detailing of all the star signs. Who knew JLo was an astrology buff?

Another thing the couple have in common is their star signs, as they’re both Leo’s. A match made in heaven! (Or the stars).

The Amazon original movie is the reimagining of the life events that inspired the music star’s new album, This Is Me…Now, the sequel to her 2002 album This Is Me…Then, released during her first relationship with the Gone Girl star.

The couple’s first relationship ‘crumbled under the pressure’

As the singer’s new project showcases her love journey with her husband, she recently opened up about why their first relationship came to an end.

Bennifer first got engaged in 2003, before calling it quits in 2004. They then mended hearts around the globe when they rekindled their relationship in 2021 and finally tied the knot in 2022.

Opening up about what went wrong the first time, the 54-year-old said their relationship “crumbled under the weight of the pressure.”

“Our relationship crumbled under the weight of the pressure,” she said in an interview with Variety. “We lost a sense of ourselves, and we needed to separate because we didn’t know how to survive it. I had to figure myself out, and he had to figure himself out.” 

However, two decades later they’re back, and better than ever!
