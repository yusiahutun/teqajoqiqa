Best black romantic comedies to watch in 2021

February 09, 2024

Many people consider the 1990s and early 2000s as the golden era for black romance on the screen. Viewers would turn on their TVs and plunge themselves in the comedy and drama plots of some shows. Great black romantic comedies like Love Jones (1997) and The Brothers (2001) received an overwhelming reception. These movies and TV shows told stories of black people from a comical and romantic point of view.

But most recently, newer shows have come up that have given black actors and actresses more freedom to express themselves. Some are excellent for their complex and original approach to love and relationships. Others have stuck to the tradition of filling living rooms with laughter

Thanks to the huge number of black romantic comedies available today, settling for a movie to watch isn't easy. That is where this list comes handy. Here are the best romantic comedy movies with black actors and actresses.

Think Like A Man is a romantic comedy film based on Steve Harvey’s best-selling book, Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man. It is one of the best black romantic comedy movies that offer a continuous laughing experience. The film revolves around four friends who conspire to reverse positions on their women after finding out they are using Steve’s book to get relationship advice against them.

Think Like A Man is a well-written romantic comedy-drama that will entertain you from the beginning to the end. It has the best cast that brings out the story to life. The direction is superb and ensures that plot lines and scenes keep moving and make the viewer interested in what will happen next after the four friends discover what their women have been doing.
