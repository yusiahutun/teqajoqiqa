Amal Clooney Goes for Gold in Her Best Jumpsuit Yet

May 12, 2024
blog

If there are any style lessons to be derived from Amal Clooney’s wardrobe, one is surely that a jumpsuit can be a girl’s best friend.

The human-rights lawyer proved the effectiveness of a good jumpsuit during her most recent public appearance in Madrid, where she spoke at the We Choose Earth Tour conference. For the occasion, Clooney relied on her go-to closet staple—with a golden twist.

She walked onstage dressed in a one-shoulder jumpsuit awash in a shiny gold hue. The statement piece featured a draped detail on the torso that twisted out into a hanging waist adornment, as well as tapered pleated pant legs. She color-coordinated her footwear with the jumpsuit, opting for transparent stiletto pumps with a gold heel and toe. For her accessories, she wore rose gold, onyx, and diamond earrings and a bracelet from Cartier.

Outside of the event, Clooney further accessorized with large black sunglasses and a tiny black croc-embossed bag.

Jumpsuits have become something of a trademark look for the lawyer.

For instance, she made an appearance last month at the international festival 4Gamechangers while wearing a smart charcoal-gray jumpsuit. The pinstriped piece included a collared V-neckline, two matching buttons on the bodice, a black leather belt at the waist, and a billowing wide-leg silhouette.

That same week, during a joint appearance with husband George Clooney at the Prince’s Trust and TKMaxx & Homesense Awards, Clooney stunned in a minimalistic beige jumpsuit with billowing pant legs and a black leather panel wrapped around the waist.
