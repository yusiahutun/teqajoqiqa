DJ Khaled's 'amazing' weight loss is down to one change in the last 2 months

July 07, 2024
blog

DJ Khaled has shown off his weight loss over the last couple of months, which is down to one lifestyle change he’s made.

We take a look at the music producer’s weight loss, and just how he achieved it, on top of making his ‘dream come true’ by opening a new store in Miami Beach.

DJ Khaled feels ‘amazing’ after his weight loss

Opening up to Page Six, Khaled revealed how he lost over 20 lbs in the last two months. By playing golf.

“My wife loves yoga. I love golf, ” he said. “So while she does yoga, I am golfing. I might eat more than I am supposed to eat — but I’ve been working on that a lot more lately. I have been feeling so amazing and inspired. I just feel great, and vibrant.”

“I was 290 about two months ago, and I am 267 today, ever since I’ve been playing golf. It may not be a lot to y’all, but it is a lot to me,” the 47-year-old continued.

However, just like everyone, Khaled still has his guilty pleasures. He told the publication that the only thing he wants to “curb” is that he loves chocolate chip cookies and Cinnamon Toast Crunch.

“That’s why I golf every day and exercise every day. So I kind of want to make sure I don’t overeat… but you know, I’m like everybody, I get excited,” he revealed.

The star opens up a store in Miami

As well as the music scene, DJ Khaled has now taken the retail scene, as he opens up a store in Miami, with his one-of-a-kind concept storefront, We The Best × SNIPES.

The Grammy Award winner recently took to his Instagram to document the grand opening of the two “powerhouses” coming together, as the outside was filled with fans waiting to get their hands on the goods.

Taking to their own Instagram on the collab, SNIPES said: “We came together with @djkhaled to bring the community and give them a dope in-store experience, including a shopping spree.”

DJ Khaled’s son comforted by Lionel Messi

Just like Kim Kardashian has become a soccer mom, it seems DJ Khaled is also a soccer dad, as his oldest son, Asahd joined Messi on pitch before the Inter Milan vs Atlanta United game last week.

However, it looked like poor Asahd was overcome with emotion as he can be seen in tears in the moment with his idol, with 27,000 eyes watching him in the stadium.

In an adorable moment, Messi, who is a father of three himself, comforted the youngster by placing his hands on his shoulders and whispering something to him, which put him at ease.
