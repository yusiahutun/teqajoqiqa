From Jennifer Lopez to Harry Styles, So Many Celebs Wore Fringe to the 2023 Grammys

May 21, 2024
blog

It's safe to assume that a lot of shaking and shimmying goes down at the Grammy Awards, whether it's in the audience as those watching groove along with the night's festivities or up on the stage as the actual performers belt their hearts out. So, it's only natural that the stars would break out the fringe. After all, it's a fun detail essentially made for movement.

That was clearly the thought process behind some of the best looks from the 2023 Grammys. While some artists showed up on the red carpet wearing fancy fringe gowns, others changed into fringe jumpsuits before entertaining the crowd.

Ahead, we're rounding up the celebrities who rocked (and rocked out in) this playful trend.

Viola Davis

This multi-colored Naeem Khan was custom-made for the actress, who became an EGOT winner thanks to winning the Grammy for Best Audiobook.

Harry Styles

If you feel like bopping around each time you hear Styles' hit, "As It Was," we don't blame you. Even the singer wore something fit for jumping, running, and dancing, taking the stage in a fringe jumpsuit while singing this song.

Jennifer Lopez

Lopez knows how to command attention at award shows, so it's no surprise that the multi-hyphenate showed up to the 2023 Grammys in a Gucci gown, complete with fringe details and a ruffled train.

Cardi B

Fringe, but with a twist. That's how we'd best describe Cardi B's Paco Rabanne ensemble, which was plucked to the archives of the late designer and worn as she took the stage to present Best Rap Album.

Heidi Klum

While most of the fringe looks from the Grammys were silver, Klum opted for gold, posing in this plunging, printed dress from The Blonds.

Miranda Lambert

Many of the night's best looks gave off a Western Glam vibe, and naturally, Lambert was right on trend with her custom Le Thanh Hoa dress, which included some extra shine at the shoulder.
