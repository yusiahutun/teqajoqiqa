Zooey Deschanel's Changing Looks

1997

The high school junior hit the heavy liner form as she called it, a "Brigitte Bardot French new wave look."

2002

The Good Girl actress sported her signature retro-chic makeup. Her tip: "MAC Goldmine [eye shadow] is good with red lipstick if you have blue eyes."

2003

"Blond hair softens me a bit, and with my pale skin it is sort of angelic-looking," said the newly lightened Elf lead.

2005

The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy beauty played up her big blue eyes with fanciful individual lashes.

2006

"I like bangs because they are cute," said the Failure to Launch star of her long fringe. They same was true of her bow-bedecked headband.

2007

Deschanel attended the premiere of Tin Man with her signature blunt fringe.

2009

"I would describe my own style as feminine, even flirtatious," Deschanel tells InStyle. For the (500) Days of Summer premiere, she added a diamond barrette from Neil Lane to a vintage-style 'do.

2010

Deschanel brought out her big blue eyes with faux lashes.

2011

Subtle amber highlights added depth to Deschanel's thick locks.

2011

At the L.A. premiere of Our Idiot Brother in Los Angeles, Deschanel channeled her inner Twiggy with her exaggerated undereye lashes.

2011

Deschanel glowed at GQ's Man of the Year Party in shimmering makeup.

2012

For the Golden Globes, Deschanel won our hearts with her mod cat-eyes courtesy of makeup artist Jorjee Douglass. Using Rimmel products, Douglass blended black and silver shadows along the lid to create the swooping shape, concentrating black shadow in the crease for dimension. A back-and-forth swipe of liquid liner along the top lashline, and a few feathery strokes of black pencil along the bottom one were the final touches. (As was a generous coating of rich mascara.)

2013

From her retro ponytail to her wild nail art, Deschanel had fun with her Golden Globes look. "We always love to go very '60s with Zooey's makeup, so we looked to Brigitte Bardot and Twiggy for inspiration," said her makeup artist Jorjee Douglass. "We went with a modern version of the look that made her blue eyes pop." Douglas layered two Tarte products to create a custom lip hue for the star, starting with the Glamazon Pure Performance Lipstick in Bold. Using her fingers, she dabbed on the coral color to create a stain, then swept the LipSurgence Lip Tint in Delighted on top.

2014

The actress swapped her signature cat-eye for long lashes and a bold lip.

2015

The actress stepped out at the New York premiere of Rock The Kasbah wearing her hair in loose waves, a casual half-up 'do, and sideswept bangs.
