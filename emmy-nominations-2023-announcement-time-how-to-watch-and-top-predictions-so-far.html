How does Emmys 2023 voting work?

The public cannot vote for their favorite shows and celebrity stars. Since the Emmy Award is a symbol of peer recognition, only members of the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences – also known as the Television Academy – can vote. The Academy has nearly 20,000 members who are divided into 31 groups of specialized fields, such as performers, music, and casting directors.

Members can only vote for nominees and winners in categories related to their area of specialty.

Can you buy tickets to the Emmy Awards and red carpet?

Show tickets cannot be purchased as they are offered on an as-available basis to Television Academy Active and Associate members and nominees.

If you want to be a seat filler or be part of the red carpet arrivals, you can sign up at the Seatfillers And More website for a chance to enter Hollywood’s most exclusive events, including The Grammys and Emmys.
