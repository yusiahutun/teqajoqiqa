An Instagram model and former influencer has come under fire for boasting about how she 'owns four houses and is buying a fifth' in response to a struggling fan asking for advice.

Kerri Gribble, 23, took to her Instagram Stories to try and relate to a 23-year-old follower who said they felt 'lost' and 'behind' in life.

She said she felt 'the same' way before going on to 'boast' she and her partner Josh Henke own four houses and are buying another.

Kerri said she tries to remember she's doing 'pretty well' in life because she can have the air-conditioning on as long as she wants and still be able to 'pay the bill for it'.

Her comments came in response to a fan, who wrote to her asking for 'advice for feeling lost in life' adding: 'I'm 23 and feel like I'm so far behind for people my age.'
