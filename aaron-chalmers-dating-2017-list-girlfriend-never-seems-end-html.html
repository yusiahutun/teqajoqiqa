MMA Fights & Height

Aaron, who has a commendable height of five feet & nine inches, also tried his luck in the event of BAMMA. He fought his first MMA fight against Greg Jenkins in the ring at Birmingham's Genting Arena on 12 May 2017.

His flying start continued as he won his first three fights for the promotion, all three of them by a first-round stoppage. However, things took a pause ever since for Aaron and his MMA career.
