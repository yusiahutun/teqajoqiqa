See Hailey Bieber Layer a Wet Tee Over a Tiny String Bikini

June 26, 2024
blog

Hailey Bieber is already providing style inspo for the summer days ahead.

The model marked Memorial Day weekend with a series of seaside pics yesterday, sharing photos from a recent yacht outing where she styled a simple white tee as a sexy coverup. In the pics, the star lounges in a berry toned bikini top and tiny string bottoms, adding a wet "Got Milk?" t-shirt tied into a semi-sheer crop top.

She finished the beachy look with a pair of chunky silver heart-shaped earrings, with slicked-back hair and a makeup-free face.

"all I know is, I’m about to have the best summer of my life," she captioned the carousel, which included snaps of the model posing on a surfboard.

The holiday weekend update comes after a busy month for the Rhode founder, who traveled to London with husband Justin Bieber to celebrate the skincare brand's U.K. launch. Hailey wore several luxe looks over the course of the trip, from strapless corseted co-ords to body-hugging turtlenecks.

For the launch party, the star made a glittering appearance in a slinky tube midi dress from N21's pre-fall 2023 collection, which was entirely covered in shimmering silver sequins. She styled the sleek statement dress with metallic gray pointed-toe pumps, and further accessorized with a plain black leather shoulder bag and chunky ribbed silver earrings.

Ricky Vigil M / Justin E Palmer//Getty Images

She was also spotted during an evening out with Justin, where she wore a black bodycon midi dress by Thierry Mugler, which hailed from the ’90s and featured velvet detailing along the shoulders, bust, and waist, cut into shapes meant to accentuate or sculpt parts of the body. She paired the dress with sheer black Calzedonia tights, glossy black pointed-toe Jimmy Choo pumps, and a small black shoulder bag.
