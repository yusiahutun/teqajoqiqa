An Update For Our Country Living Fair Community

blog

To our Country Living Fair Community,

After careful deliberation and in following guidance from local, state, and national authorities, we have made the difficult decision to cancel the Country Living Fairs for 2022. We are disappointed, but our first priority is the health and well-being of our Country Living community.

On an exciting note, we are working on plans for more fairs in additional markets as soon as it’s safe to do so as well as opportunities to connect virtually. Details will be announced soon! (Be sure to follow us @CountryLivingFair and check Fair.CountryLiving.com for the latest information!)

We greatly appreciate your understanding and look forward to gathering soon.

Thank you and stay safe.

Rachel Barrett is the Editor-in-Chief of Country Living. She can't pass up a vintage seascape, drives an '89 Woody Wagoneer (that is, when it'll start), and hopes to buy you a lemonade at a future Country Living Fair.
