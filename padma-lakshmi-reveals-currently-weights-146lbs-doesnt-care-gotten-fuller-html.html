Padma Lakshmi has gained weight after shooting Bravo's Top Chef for the past several months.

But the 49-year-old former Vogue model says she just does not care. 

'I haven't had time to diet so I'm about 146 lbs. right now, but I'm okay,' she told Us Weekly while at the Emmys on Sunday. 'I'm lucky because I gain weight exponentially, like everywhere. So I don't care.'

'It's fine. I mean, I don't even have a bra on, so. I just think the female body is beautiful in its natural state,' she said.

'I don't believe that we all have to do things to make it smooth and beautiful. At the end of the day, who cares?'

Earlier she had said that always dieting is a bad message for her daughter Krishna.

'She's noticed, and suddenly she's told me and others in our circle, "I don't want to eat because I'm watching my figure," or, "I weigh too much,"' Lakshmi previously wrote in The Hollywood Reporter. 

'Her comments stopped me dead in my tracks. Her words scared me. Language matters. We send signals to our daughters every day. And I am her first touchstone of femininity.'

She also said that one day she hopes the red carpet isn’t nearly as important as 'making sure my daughter doesn’t measure her worth by her dress size.'

The mom of one's show Top Chef is nominated for the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Competition Program alongside American Ninja Warrior, RuPaul's Drag Race, Nailed It!, The Voice and The Amazing Race.

Lakshmi was also nominated for a Primetime Outstanding Reality Host Emmy back in 2009. 

It was announced in July that she will be at the center of a new TV series alongside Hulu Kitchen. 

The streaming giant is putting out a 10-episode untitled series with the executive producer in the form of a 30-minute show that will follow her as she treks across America to sample a diverse selection of cultural food.
