GMB fans blown away by 'glorious' Susanna Reid as she dazzles in regal outfit

July 13, 2024
blog

Good Morning Britain viewers were left in awe of "glorious" Susanna Reid as she wore a dress "fit for the occasion" on the special Coronation episode.

The ITV favourite hosted a special Saturday episode (May 6) ahead of King Charles III's Royal Coronation, and really got into the spirit with her outfit.

The 52-year-old wore a form-fitting dress made up of a gold patterned fabric, with statement ruffled sleeves and a plunging neckline.

The regal colour complimented her honey-highlighted hair beautifully, and it seemed Susanna was feeling confident as she flashed a wide smile to the camera.

She said on Instagram: "Coronation day. An honour to report on this historic occasion."

Describing her dress as "fit for the occasion", she told fans it was from Isabell Kristensen Couture, though fans hoping to recreate the look will have to splash out £2,400.

One person told Susanna she looked "glorious", while she was also showered with compliments like "ravishing" and "stunning".

Someone else commented: "You look absolutely beautiful – your hair, the dress."

Another said: "Looking fantastic in that dress."

One follower wrote: "You look stunning! Love the hair."

Meanwhile viewers on Twitter were equally as impressed, with one saying: "I will say Susanna Reid looks absolutely incredible, that gold dress is a serve!"

Another said: "Wow Susanna is looking fabulous on GMB."

And a third wrote: "Susanna's gold dress really compliment her flawless complexion."

During the episode, fans were left confused as they heard someone label someone else a "bitch" off-camera, with some assuming it was Susanna speaking.

However broadcaster Petronalla Wyatt, who had been on the show, admitted to the blunder.

She wrote on Twitter: "Oops. Was off air this morning @GMB when a mike picked me up having a private chat about how a royal had been unfairly smeared in some quarters as a b.... apologies if anyone thought it referred to them. It didn't!"

Good Morning Britain airs weekdays at 6am on ITV
