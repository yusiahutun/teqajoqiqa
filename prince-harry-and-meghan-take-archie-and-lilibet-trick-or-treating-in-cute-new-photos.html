Prince Harry and Meghan take Archie and Lilibet 'trick or treating' in cute new photos

May 30, 2024

Harry and Meghan Markle’s Halloween plans included taking Prince Archie and Princess Lilibet trick-or-treating.

Halloween always marks a special occasion for Prince Harry and Meghan Markle. Right before their relationship became public knowledge in 2016, Harry, his cousin Princess Eugenie, and her now-husband Jack Brooksbank had a fun pre-Halloween celebration with Meghan Markle. Hours before the news of their new relationship was going to be splashed on front pages, they wanted to make sure they enjoyed “one final, fun night out.” Now, seven years later, the special day was filled with adorable family memories with their little ones.

Prince Harry and Meghan take Archie and Lilibet ‘trick or treating’

Harry and Meghan were seen on a fun-filled trick-and-treat walk with Prince Archie and Princess Lilibet. The Duke and Duchess of Sussex chose to don casual attires, grey and black tees and denim. Lilibet donned a fluffy pink costume and Archie was seen holding a pumpkin tray along with dad Harry.

The royal couple wore hats to avoid attention. They reportedly took their children out for Halloween along with other families near to their Montecito mansion. The picture was posted on Elon Musk’s X (formerly Twitter) where fans could not help but soon at the adorable family photo of the celebrity couple with their little ones.

Prince Harry and Meghan’s ‘bizarre costume’ Halloween

Harry and Meghan love Halloween and their ‘bizarre’ costume party was proof that they enjoyed the normalcy of festivities away from the limelight and media attention.

Right before their relationship went public, Meghan talked about how Harry came to Toronto to enjoy Halloween with the former TV star as she filmed Suits. The celebrity couple was joined by cousin Eugenie, and her partner Jack, and photos from the night showed the royals can also let loose to enjoy the true spirits of the festive season.

Speaking of their Halloween bash, Meghan revealed on The Ellen DeGeneres Show how they snuck out to have a fun night out.

“He came to see me in Toronto and our friends and his cousin Eugenie and now her husband Jack, they came as well,” Meghan said.

“And the four of us snuck out in Halloween costumes to just have one fun night on the town before it was out in the world that we were a couple. It was a post-apocalypse theme. So we had all this very bizarre costuming on and we were able to just sort of have one final fun night out.”
