Liam Payne delights Instagram fans with bed selfie

April 12, 2024
blog

Liam Payne has sent his social media page into overdrive by posting an intimate photograph of himself in bed. The One Direction singer shared the personal snap in the early hours of Saturday morning and his 13.3 million Instagram followers adored the picture of the good looking star. In the black and white photo, Liam lies in bed without a shirt, his eyes closed and his shoulder tattoo on show. He simply captioned the photo: “Zzzzzzzzz”. The snap got an incredible 641,553 likes in just eight hours!

Liam’s followers were quick to comment on the picture. One wrote: “Do you know that you're so handsome?” while another flattered the star, “You look so good.” A third fan sympathised with the singer over the exhaustion faced by new parents, saying: “Lol lots of sleepless nights in the early days, get it while you can!” Another commented: “You lookin' too tired luv, sleep well.” There were more questions for the star on the name of his newborn son, which is yet to be announced. One follower asked: “What`s the name of your baby boy?” And more than one person teased Liam over how he managed to take a selfie while asleep. “How did you take this photo of your sleeping?” asked a fan.

New parents Liam Payne and Cheryl

Liam and his partner Cheryl welcomed their son into the world five weeks ago. Cheryl has stayed out of the public eye while she bonds with their baby boy at their Surrey home. Liam returned home from his LA trip this week, where he has been working on his new album. On Wednesday, the star posted a photo to Instagram showing himself partying at a Migos gig at London’s Brixton Academy. He recently opened about becoming a father to Pedro Ramirez while in LA. Liam told him: "He's great, he's good. I love him and I miss him a lot," he said. Another fan claimed that Liam said his baby boy, whose name is yet to be announced, is "doing well".

Cheryl's beautiful photo of her partner Liam Payne cradling their new born baby son has become her most popular Instagram post to date. It is the first of the Fight for this Love singer's Instagram posts to have reached over a million likes.
