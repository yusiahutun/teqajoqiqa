Man Loses KSh 300k Job after Friend He Helped Reported Him to Mzungu Boss

May 10, 2024
blog
  • A Nigerian man has lost his source of livelihood after a friend snitched on him to his Mzungu employer
  • After getting the KSh 302,600 writing job, the kindhearted fellow decided to help a jobless fellow who reached out to him on X (formerly Twitter)
  • The Nigerian man accepted to help him by outsourcing his writing job, only to be played at the end

A Nigerian lady, Adeife Adeoye, has narrated how her friend lost his writing job paying $2,000 (KSh 302,600) after a friend snitched on him.

Adeoye said a man without a job reached out to her friend on X, begging him to be assisted with work.

Her kindhearted friend agreed and decided to outsource his writing gig to the jobless fellow in return for cash.

How friend pulled a fast one on him

A disappointed Adeoye said the fellow reached out to her friend's Mzungu boss and exposed how the writing job was being outsourced to him and the pay involved.
