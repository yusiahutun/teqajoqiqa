How to apply for PSV license in Kenya: PSV badge application step-by-step guide

February 24, 2024
blog

The Kenyan National Transport and Safety Authority (NTSA) was established on 26th October 2012 through an Act of Parliament. Its main mission is to improve the accessibility of Kenyan roads for all users. The vision of NTSA is to offer efficient, reliable, and safe roads for Kenyans. The Public Service Vehicle badge has been made mandatory in the country for all drivers of public transport vehicles. Below is a step-by-step guide on the NTSA PSV badge application process.

The main purpose of NTSA is to implement policies that are related to road transport and road safety, to ensure there is the provision of safe and reliable transport services for the public, implementation of policies related to road safety and transport, and to offer a recommendation on issues relating to safety and transport.

NTSA PSV badge application online

NTSA offers several services to Kenyans, such as the NTSA taxi PSV badge application. The application forms are available online on the NTSA website and can be filled out and submitted online.

To be awarded a PSV badge, you need to have a driving license and be registered by NTSA. Below is a guide on applying for a driving license in Kenya.

  • Visit the official NTSA eCitizen website.
  • After reading all the guidelines and instructions, click "Apply Now.
  • Click on "Create an account", and you will be directed to another page.
  • Create an account.
  • A new page will appear; enter your identification number, name, and email address, and upload a passport-sized photo.
  • You will have successfully created your account.
  • Log in to your newly created account now.
  • Select the approved licencing status, click on the new application, and then select the application type.
  • Fill out the generated form with the necessary information.
  • Proceed to payment after selecting a payment method.
  • Click the confirm icon, then the submit button.
  • Once completed, print your application and submit it to the collection point to collect your licence.

    How do I get a PSV badge?

    To apply for a Public Service Vehicle badge in Kenya, follow the steps outlined below.

  • Visit the NTSA official website.
  • Just below the "Quick Links" section, click on "PSV Badge application."
  • You will be directed to a new page to log in using your email address, mobile number, and national identification number.
  • After logging in, you can complete the PSV badge form with the necessary information and submit it.

    • The PSV badge form is divided into three sections. The applicant must complete the first section. It necessitates information such as your name, gender, identification number, and designation.

    Section two allows you to fill out either part (a), which should be filled out by the employer, or part (b), which should be filled out by the company.

    Section three is only for official purposes.

    What is a driving license?

    This is the documentation that allows the holder to operate various types of motor vehicles on the road.

    How much does a PSV badge cost?

    You must pay a fee of Ksh.625 to proceed. The payment is made to the Road Transport Department in Nairobi.

    How long does PSV badge take?

    After submitting your application, you will receive your badge within five working days.

    Who requires a PSV license in Kenya?

    Matatu, taxi and uber drivers, institutional drivers, and truck drivers all require a PSV licence in Kenya.
