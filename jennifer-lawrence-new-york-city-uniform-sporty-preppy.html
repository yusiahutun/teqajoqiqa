Jennifer Lawrence gives her off-duty uniform a sporty twist

October 06, 2024
blog

Jennifer Lawrence has become known for her quietly luxurious off-duty style, where she chooses high-end but low-key pieces from brands like The Row. But, her latest street-style look sees her embrace a sportier aesthetic.

The actress was photographed while out for a walk in New York City on Wednesday, looking extremely chic in a dressed-up take on athleisure. She wore a green cashmere cardigan with white buttons, Adidas’ classic black tracksuit bottoms (with the brand’s three stripes in white on the sides) and a long black leather blazer. She stayed comfortable in a pair of black and white Vans sneakers, wore her hair straight down and went make-up free.

Related Story

Lawrence capped off her casual look with several New Yorker must-haves designed to, essentially, make it easy to avoid interacting with others: giant square tortoiseshell sunglasses, Apple AirPods, and a cap — hers a black Shania Twain cap with the singer’s name in huge yellow letters.

Lawrence has been spotted out and about in New York City the past few weeks, as she works on her many upcoming projects, including a true-story drama called Burial Rites, and a new adaptation of John Steinbeck’s 1952 novel East of Eden.
