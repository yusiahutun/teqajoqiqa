Kris Jenner Throws a Lavish Easter Bash for the Kardashian Kids

June 20, 2024
blog

Momager Kris Jenner was momaging Saturday—she threw the whole Kardashian clan a huge Easter bash.

When it comes to the Kardashian-Jenner family, everything is “go big or go home,” but especially holidays (need we remind you of Kim Kardashian’s lavish Christmas Eve party?), and Kris went all out. The party’s family-fun activities included a slime station full of Easter-themed charms, glitter, glue, scents, and huge bowls; a table of duck, bunny, lamb, and bear plushies to choose from and racks of clothes and accessories to dress them in; an egg-coloring stop full of markers; and a table full of paint to color Easter Bunny nutcrackers.

courtesy of Kim Kardashian via Instagram

courtesy of Kim Kardashian via Instagram

Kris also set up a full spread of Easter-themed treats, including milkshakes, cupcakes, cookies, Rice Krispie treats, massive cakes, and bowls of candy, all decorated in pretty pastel colors and Easter motifs; the decor followed suit. All the Kardashian kids wore adorable bunny aprons as they got crafty, and for a final personalized touch, Kris had a whole display of Easter baskets, each holding a large brown egg, for each member of the family.

Kim and Khloé Kardashian both took to Instagram to share photos from the happy day and express gratitude for their mom as a host. Kim wrote, “How cute is my mom 🐰” over a Story and “Our kids are so lucky to have the best grandma,” over another.

courtesy of Khloe Kardashian via Instagram

courtesy of Khloe Kardashian via Instagram

In her own Story, Khloe shared a close-up snap of bright yellow duck plates and matching napkins with the caption, “My mom is the most amazing human being in the entire world!!!!! It’s a fantasy over here always @krisjenner.”

Khloe also commemorated the day with a carousel of images, in which she captured smiley photos of her kids, True, five, and Tatum, one; Kim’s kids Chicago, six, and Psalm, four; herself; and her mom getting artistic. Kris was the most stylish gracious and stylish host in a baby-pink sweatsuit.
