Latest Ankara styles for men: ideas for plain and pattern designs

February 08, 2024
blog

Ankara refers to the native wear made from African printed fabric. These may include shoes, trousers, dresses, jackets, hats, and shorts. The style has been in existence since the 19th century and has gained more popularity over the last few decades. While it was typically associated with women, Ankara is now donned by all genders. So, what are some of the latest Ankara styles for men?

The Ankara men's wear is loved for its colourful African print and is deeply associated with African culture and dressing. One thing that makes the Ankara fabric a standout is the intensity of its prints; it doesn't fade like the typical printed textiles.

Men's Ankara styles for shirts

Ankara shirts for men are currently among the supper stylish tops. Why? It's because they can be rocked in almost any event, i.e., to church, work, or date. There are several fashionable Ankara styles when it comes to shirts. Here are the best styles to try.

Long sleeve shirt (plain sleeves)

This is the perfect design for someone looking for a short with just a hint of Ankara. The sleeves are made from plain coloured cotton or polyester, while the rest of the shirt has floral or patterned fabric with a colour palette closely matching that of the sleeves.

Half plain, half Ankara

This one takes the Ankara and plain fabric combination even further. One side of the shirt is made from a plain colour fabric while the other half is made from Ankara. In addition, you can have the sleeves in plain or patterned designs.

Long sleeve full Ankara

If you are not looking for something too fancy, a long sleeve full Ankara shirt will definitely do the trick. However, unlike the two options above, this one is made from the same material from top to bottom.

Short sleeve round collar

Nothing screams comfort like a shirt whose design is inspired by the ever-so-comfortable t-shirt. This design incorporates short sleeves and a round collar for that timeless look that can be worn to work or to informal events.

Short sleeve Mandarian collar

The Mandarian collar adds some flair to the somewhat conventional round-neck shirts. This collar has a thin opening from the collar that extends a few inches toward the chest. Again, you can choose to have yours with or without buttons.

Jackets

The modern Ankara jackets are trendy, simple, and cute. They come full of colours, making their unique designs worth purchasing. You can rock them with anything, including jeans, trousers, shorts, Khaki, etc.

Different halves

This is one of the best ways to pull off the native African fabric look. Have a patterned fabric on one side of the jacket zipper and a different one on the other side. To enhance the look further, you can go for complementary colours on the two sides.

Bold colours

Bold colours and patterns are among the defining features of African printed fabric. Choose a material with huge floral or abstract patterns and have a waist-length jacket made from it. If you are fine with bright-coloured clothes, the bold patterns or flowers in this look will definitely turn heads.

Similar halves

This is the opposite of the different halves design. If you are the type to choose simplicity over sophistication, then the similar halves jacket is for you. The look is achieved by making the jacket from a single piece of material. This means when the zipper is open, the jacket's two halves look the same.

Ankara and leather

Leather and faux leather have often been used in the clothing industry. The Ankara and leather combination is achieved by sewing a bit of printed fabric onto a leather jacket. This gives the otherwise boring-looking leather a splash of colour.

A hood on top of a jacket is not only stylish but also remarkably functional. If you are looking for an Ankara jacket for cold weather, why not opt for the hooded jacket? You could have the entire jacket in Ankara and the hood in a plain fabric or the other way around.
