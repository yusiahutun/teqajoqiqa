Paul Casey | Controversy

When asked about how he felt about the US Ryder Cup team during an interview with the Sunday Times newspaper in November 2004, Paul Casey reportedly said, “Oh, we properly hate them. We wanted to beat them as badly as possible”.

Moreover, he added that the U.S. fans could be “bloody annoying,” even though his then-coach and his then-girlfriend were both Americans.

His statement stirred up controversy in the organization. But when asked if he should apologize for the comment he made, Casey said, “Maybe explain.”

However, he accepted the fact that the use of such a strong word as “hate” was an error.
