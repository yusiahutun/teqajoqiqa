This Tom Ford candle is selling out thanks to Harry Styles

September 18, 2024
blog

After the sell-out success of Gwyneth Paltrow's infamous Goop candle, 'This Smells Like My Vagina', comes another A list-linked scented burner breaking the internet. This time, it's said to smell like Harry Styles – and thankfully not his intimate body parts.

Until recently, not many knew that Tom Ford Beauty has a candle version of the Private Blend Tobacco Vanille – the unisex scent that Styles is said to wear. But it's hot property right now thanks to a dupe version that's sold out at Target stores in the US, which caused a frenzy among fans of the pop idol.

Tom Ford's affection for London inspired the Tobacco Vanille scent, reminiscent of an English gentlemen's club, redolent with spice. We imagine Style scents his home in the same way.

"I mean I think everyone loves scented candles," he told Nick Grimshaw, adding "I usually take one from home if I’m travelling".
