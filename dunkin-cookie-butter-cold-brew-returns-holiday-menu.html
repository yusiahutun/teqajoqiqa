Dunkin' drops two new holiday flavors and fans say Starbucks is losing me

February 26, 2024
blog

DUNKIN' is kicking off the holiday celebration early with the launch of a special seasonal menu.

Fans are rejoicing as the donut shop brings back the Cookie Butter Cold Brew drink.

The drink was an instant hit last year, featuring Dunkin's ultra-smooth cold brew with notes of brown sugar and buttery cookies, topped with cookie butter cold foam and cookie butter crumbles.

Customers took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to share their excitement about the return of the drink.

"Dunkin's seasonal cookie butter cold brew is back and you already know what I'm going to be drinking everyday until it fades into the abyss again," wrote one person.

Another said: "Starbucks is losing me to Dunkin' Donuts for the next two months, they're bringing back their cookie butter cold brew!"

"I'm officially become a Starbucks girly but once dunkin' brings back that cookie butter cold brew...STARBUCKS WHO???" Added a third person.

Dunkin' is expanding on the cookie craze with the all-new Spiced Cookie coffee. The drink has flavors of brown sugar and vanilla paired with creamy oat milk to mimic the taste of a freshly baked oatmeal cookie.

Also making a comeback is the Peppermint Mocha Signature Latte and the Toasted White Chocolate Signature Latte.

Fans can also order the brand new Loaded Hash Browns, which feature a layer of hash browns topped with warm cheddar queso, a hint of jalapeno, and crispy bacon crumbles.

Those with a sweet tooth can try the triple chocolate muffin that is packed with chocolate chips and chunks.

DUNKIN' AT HOME

Dunkin' just launched a new way for fans to enjoy its coffee.

Customers bought over 6 million salted caramel cold brews over the summer.

Unfortunately, it was part of a seasonal menu and was ultimately pulled from rotation.

But Dunkin' couldn't let down loyal customers, so it launched a special salted caramel creamer that is available at grocery stores.

“From fan-favorite Dunkin’ Extra Extra Creamer to limited-time seasonal creamers, Dunkin’ knows the importance that flavor plays in a morning cup of coffee, and so does Danone,” said Kallie Goodwin, Vice President of Marketing, Coffee Creamers for Danone North America.

“We are thrilled to introduce Salted Caramel Creamer to the Dunkin’ portfolio, giving fans another restaurant-inspired creamer to enjoy. Salted Caramel has been such a success in stores, so we wanted to bring that same excitement to the at-home coffee experience, as well.”

It is described as a "sweet and subtle" flavor that fans will immediately recognize.
