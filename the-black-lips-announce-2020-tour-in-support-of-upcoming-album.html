The Black Lips Announce 2020 Tour in Support of Upcoming Album

May 09, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – The Black Lips have announced a tour in support of their upcoming album, Sing in a World That’s Falling Apart.

Scheduled to kick off on January 21 in Gainesville, FL, the tour will make stops in Philadelphia, Brooklyn, Toronto, Detroit, Chicago, Nashville, and more, before wrapping up in Atlanta, GA, on March 14.

The band is currently overseas for a block of UK/European dates slated to wrap on November 23. They will also perform a handful of shows in Texas before the new year.
