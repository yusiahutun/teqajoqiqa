50 Mother's Day Messages for Friends and Family 2023

March 15, 2024
blog

Whether intended for use on a card, in a text, or as a declaration on social media, Mother's Day messages can be a tricky business. There's a lot of pressure involved—after all, your mom is not only the heart and soul of your family, she's one of the very most important people in your life. That's why, along with celebrating her on the second Sunday in May with homemade gifts, Mom-themed movie viewing, or even a special cocktail, you're definitely going to want to go the extra mile and jot down a few well-chosen sentences telling her how much she means to you. To help get those creative juices flowing, we've put together the following list of Mom's Day messages sure to inspire your inner wordsmith.

Do you have a mama who loves to laugh? We have plenty of cheeky, comical messages, along with sweet sentiments sure to bring a tear to her eye if she's the tenderhearted type. We've also included inspirational quotes, complimentary tidings, pretty poems and moving Bible verses about mothers. Then, after you've added a few thoughts of your own to these suggestions (perhaps around a cherished memory of your mom, or something you're particularly grateful to her for), check out our list of thoughtful and fun Mother's Day activities and plan something you two can do together.
