'The Brady Bunch' Cast Then and Now: Barry Williams and More

October 05, 2024
blog

Though it seems like just yesterday that The Brady Bunch first premiered, it’s been more than 50 years since the iconic series first graced our TV screens in 1969. A lot has changed over the decades, but the beloved cast of Barry Williams, Maureen McCormick, Christopher Knight and more stars are still iconic today.

The long-running series told the story … of a man named Mike Brady … who became a widower and single father of his three sons, Greg, Peter and Boddy. After meeting and falling in love with Carol, who is the mom of Marcia, Jan and Cindy, the couple married and blended their broods to become a family of eight.

The Brady Bunch ran for five seasons from September 1969 to March 1974. In addition to Barry, who portrayed Greg; Maureen, who played Marcia; and Christopher, who appeared as Peter; the cast also included Susan Olsen (Cindy), Eve Plumb (Jan) and Mike Lookinland (Bobby). We can’t forget about late stars Florence Henderson and Robert Reed, who played the roles of Carol and Mike, respectively.

While it’s been decades since the original Brady Bunch aired on TV, the comedy sitcom has inspired a plethora of spin-offs, including The Brady Kids from 1972 to 1973, The Brady Bunch Hour from 1976 to 1977 and The Brady Bunch 35th Anniversary Reunion Special in 2004.

Most recently, the cast got together for the HGTV series A Very Brady Renovation in 2019. The six remaining members joined forces to renovate the iconic real-life Brady Bunch home in Los Angeles that was used to film throughout the program.

At the time the cast members reunited for A Very Brady Renovation, Maureen marveled over the exciting moment, calling it an “out-of-body experience” during a chat with Closer Weekly. “[It’s] very surreal,” she exclusively gushed in September 2019.

“Everyone lives in different parts of the country and everyone was working on different parts of the room or in different rooms, so we weren’t together constantly at all,” she told Closer. “I think at least 15 years since we had all been together on camera.”

Though it’s not always easy to get the gang together, Maureen and some of her castmates reunited in March 2021. Christopher shared a sweet photo of the group, which included himself and his wife, Cara; Maureen and her husband, Michael Cummings; Barry and Susan. “Dinner last night at @eatatbasta with family,” he captioned the post, noting they’re “missing” Eve and her husband, Ken Pace; Barry’s wife, Tina Mahina; and Mike and his spouse, Kelly Wermuth.

Hopefully, they can all reunite again soon!
