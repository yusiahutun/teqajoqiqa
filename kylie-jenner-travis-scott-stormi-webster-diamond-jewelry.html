Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Wear Matching Diamonds Cut Into Stormi's Name

August 08, 2024
blog

Parents Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott debuted their newest diamond jewelry on Instagram yesterday, and the pieces are a super sparkly tribute to their 8-month-old daughter Stormi Webster. Kylie first shared a video of her wrist, clad with a diamond watch a tennis bracelet that has her baby girl's name spelled out in dangling charms. "Diamonds cut into Stormi," she wrote.

Jenner then shared a close-up snap of Travis' neck, which is heavily layered with diamond chains. But if you look closely, you'll notice Stormi's name appears on one of his necklaces. "I spy," Kylie captioned the photo.

The new mom is clearly a fan of personalized jewelry, and even got baby Stormi her own tiny gold bracelet with her name engraved on it.

It's possible Kylie and Travis' customized bling was inspired by a ring that big sister Khloé Kardashian owns, which features her daughter True's name spelled out in diamonds.

Jenner, Scott, and baby Stormi recently spent the day on an adorable autumnal outing as a family. Kylie shared photos from the day, which marked her daughter's first visit to a pumpkin patch. The little tot rocked a Louis Vuitton Supreme t-shirt, camouflage pants, and black sneakers for the trip.

Kylie recently opened about her daughter during a YouTube makeup tutorial with James Charles. "She's just changing every day," Kylie said. "She's really just becoming a little person. She reminds me a lot of Kendall."

The first-time mom also said that she is definitely considering having more kids. "I do want to have more," she told Charles. "But when is the question." She also said that she wants to give Stormi a sister. "Like, I want seven girls and then like maybe I'll consider a boy."
