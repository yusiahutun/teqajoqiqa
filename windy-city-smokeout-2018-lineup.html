Windy City Smokeout Announces 2018 Lineup

blog

The BBQ, craft beer and country music festival returns to Chicago from July 13–15, 2018, for the sixth consecutive year. According to a press release, organizers expect more than 50,000 fans on Grand Avenue near the Chicago River for the three-day event this year.

Chicago barbecue joint Bub City and ACM Award-winning venue Joe’s Bar are hosting the Windy City Smokeout in 2018, and the festival once again features a wide variety of craft beers. The 2018 pitmaster lineup will feature top talent from Chicago and around the country. Windy City Smokeout will also offer non-BBQ options, including savory bites from the Budlong, Bub City Burger Shack, Barrel & Ashes, Sir Kensington’s and Mac Shack, sweets from Bang Bang Pie & Biscuits and specialty cocktails from Three Dots & a Dash and Casamigos.

CMA New Artist of the Year nominee Young heads up a Friday night (July 13) lineup that also includes Drake White, Ryan Kinder and more. ACM Awards nominee Eldredge headlines Saturday night (July 14) after a day of music from artists including Aaron Lewis and Ashley McBryde, while the Grammy-nominated Brothers Osborne lead a Sunday (July 15) lineup that includes hot rising star Walker Hayes. See the full lineup below.

Festival passes are currently available at www.windycitysmokeout.com. Prices begin at $40 for a general admission day pass and $110 for a general admission three-day pass, with VIP passes priced at $175 for a single day and $509 for a three-day pass.
