Marc Jacobs Launches Buzzy New Fragrance

March 14, 2024
blog

Media Platforms Design Team

Marc Jacobs has added another fragrance to his beauty coven. A delicious marriage of fruity and floral notes, Honey reimagines big sister Dot's graphic ladybug motif with a sweet honeybee-inspired bottle — a nod to the fragrance's creamy peach nectar and honey elements. Appropriately, a whiff of Honey feels a bit like inhaling sunlight — perfect for a warm summer's afternoon or as a lovely reminder of one when things cool down.

Marc Jacobs Honey Eau de Parfum Spray, $92 for 3.4 oz, sephora.com.
