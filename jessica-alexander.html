Clips of her scenes have gone viral on TikTok, with millions of views and comments. Early Life

Jessica Alexander entered the world on the 19th of June in 1999, in Westminster, London, UK. She is of British nationality and the Christian religion. Her zodiac sign is Gemini. Jessica Alexander Career

Jessica Alexander is a performer and model with experience in the entertainment sector starting in 2013. Her career commenced by portraying characters in short films, including Necktie and Truck. She made her television debut in 2018, playing Lucy Carpenter in the Italian Disney Channel series Penny on M.A.R.S.

Jessica gained recognition for her role as Olivia Hayes in the B.B.C. iPlayer series Get Even in 2020, where she played a popular but closeted bisexual teenager. She then starred in several films, such as Glasshouse, A Banquet, and Into the Deep, showcasing her range and versatility.

Her role brought her to fame as Vanessa, the human alter-ego of Ursula, in Disney’s live-action adaptation of The Little Mermaid in 2023. Her performance received excellent reviews and became a viral sensation on TikTok. She has also been cast in the Globoplay series Fallen, based on the novel series by Lauren Kate.
