So, which team has the largest fanbase in the NFL? Sportskeeda has an interesting take on this topic and shared an interesting breakdown of the most adored teams.

1. Dallas Cowboys

Dallas Cowboys, the reigning champions of the NFL, has the most fans. Missing a seat at the prestigious AT&T stadium during games is not strange.

The Dallas Cowboys also has the most followed Facebook account, boasting over 8 million followers. The team was the first in the U.S. to generate $620 million in revenue, making Forbes' list of the most valued NFL teams for the 12th year straight.

Fans don't love the Dallas Cowboys for no reason. The team makes an immense effort to deliver the best. They have made it to the Super Bowl eighteen times, winning five appearances. On top of this, the Dallas Cowboys is the only team to record a winning streak of 20 consecutive seasons (1966-85).

2. New England Patriots

New England Patriots comes second among the most popular NFL teams worldwide. The football team is based in the Greater Boston area.

The New England Patriots was formed in 1959 as the Boston Patriots, and it has evolved over the years to become one of the best in the NFL. The team holds the record for the most Super Bowl wins (6, tied with the Pittsburgh Steelers), appearances (11), and losses (5, tied with the Denver Broncos).

The New England Patriots have over 5 million followers on Instagram and are the most popular NFL team on Instagram.

3. Pittsburgh Steelers

The Pittsburgh Steelers was created in 1933 and is among the most successful NFL teams. Interestingly, the NFL team has the same number of Super Bowl victories as the New England Patriots (6). The team has also played in 16 AFC Championship games, hosted 11 as many conference title games, and won eight AFC Championships.

Pittsburgh Steelers has over 4 million followers on Instagram, which shows that they have a massive fanbase not only in the United States of America but in other parts of the world.

4. Green Bay Packers

Green Bay Packers is another NFL team with the most loyal fans. It is the third-oldest American football team, dating back to 1919. It is the only non-profit, community-owned major league professional sports team based in the United States of America.

Green Bay Packers has won 13 titles overall in NFL history. No other franchise has won more championships than the team. The Green Bay Packers were the only NFL team to defeat the AFL before the AFL-NFL merger.

The team holds an annual scrimmage called Family Night at Lambeau Field. During 2004 and 2005, sell-out a crowd of over 60,000 fans appeared, with the highest number in 2005 of 62,492 people when the Buffalo Bills appeared.

5. Denver Broncos

The Denver Broncos was established on August 14, 1959, meaning they have a history of more than six decades. The team is headquartered in Dove Valley, Colorado. Forbes valued the team at $5.1 billion in 2022, making it the 12th most-valuable team in the NFL.

The Denver Broncos have won three Super Bowls and eight AFC titles. The team has over 1.4 million followers on Instagram.

6. San Francisco 49ers

The San Francisco 49ers are another NFL team with the most international fans. The team has immense accomplishments and titles, with five Super Bowl victories and legendary players like Joe Montana and Stee Young.

The team was created in 1946 as an All-America Football Conference (AAFC) charter member and joined the NFL in 1949. According to Forbes, the San Francisco 49ers has an estimated value of $9 billion. In 2020, the team was ranked the 12th most valuable sports team in the world, behind the Los Angeles Rams and above the Chicago Bears.

7. Kansas City Chiefs

The Kansas City Chiefs is an American football team based in Kansas City, Missouri. The team has over three million followers on Instagram. Patrick Mahomes has much to do with the team’s popularity, considering he is the best quarterback in the league.

8. Philadelphia Eagles

The Philadelphia Eagles are based in Philadelphia, the United States of America, and over the years, the team has become one of the most popular. The team was established in 1933 as a replacement for the bankrupt Frankford Yellow Jackets when a group led by Bert Bell secured the rights to an NFL Franchise in Philadelphia.

Eagle's stadium, Lincoln Financial Field, is a must-see to experience the best of the NFL. The Philadelphia Eagles have over 2.9 million Instagram followers.

9. New York Giants

The New York Giants joined the NFL in 1925, and they are the only one of that group still existing, as well as the longest-established team in the North-eastern United States of America. The team has a massive following on Instagram, with over 2.7 million followers. They are four-time champions.

Even though they relocated from Oakland to Las Vegas, their fans have always been there, giving their support.
