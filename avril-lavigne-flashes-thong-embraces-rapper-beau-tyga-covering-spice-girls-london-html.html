Avril Lavigne flashes thong and embraces rapper beau Tyga... before covering Spice Girls in London

August 22, 2024
blog

Eight-time Grammy nominee Avril Lavigne flashed her thong in an Instagram slideshow she posted Thursday captioned: 'Lyfe lately.'

In one shot, the Canadian 38-year-old embraced her new boyfriend - Grammy nominee Tyga - whom she was first pictured with at Nobu Malibu on February 19.

The 33-year-old SoCal native (born Micheal Ray Stevenson) has been keeping Avril company during her 48-date Love Sux Tour, which concluded in London on Wednesday.

'It's crazy,' Lavigne - who boasts 87.9M social media followers - said via Instastory.

'We're about to go onstage and I'm kind of sad because it's the last day of the world tour.'

Whale tail: Eight-time Grammy nominee Avril Lavigne flashed her thong in an Instagram slideshow she posted Thursday

During the European leg of her tour, the I'm a Mess rocker has been rocking faux facial piercings as well as the $80K diamond chain necklace Tyga bought for her from jeweler Eric Mavani.

For her last concert at England's Alexandra Palace, Avril - who's signed to Travis Barker's label DT Records - and tourmate Phem interrupted their set filled with pop-punk songs to belt the Spice Girls' 1996 hit Wannabe.

Lavigne's romance with the half-Vietnamese hip-hop star blindsided her ex-fiancé Mod Sun, whom she'd been engaged to for 10 months after meeting while recording their 2021 duet Flames.

The Love It When You Hate Me singer previously divorced Nickelback frontman Chad Kroeger in 2015 after two years of marriage, and she divorced Sum 41 frontman Deryck Whibley in 2010 after three years of marriage.

Tyga - who dated Kylie Jenner when she was 16 - is father to 10-year-old son King Cairo Stevenson from his nearly three-year relationship with ex-fiancée Blac Chyna, which ended in 2014.

The Day One rapper - who hasn't shared any snaps of Avril on his Instagram account - worryingly only received one-year probation in February 2022 for alleged domestic violence against his ex Camaryn Swanson.

Tyga is next scheduled to perform with Grammy nominee Marshmello on May 28 at XS Nightclub's 'Sunday Nightswim' party in Las Vegas.
