    Michelle Keegan is hailed for her flawless skin, incredible cheekbones and her plump pout – so we reckon fans will be thrilled to find out that one of her favourite beauty products is a bit of a bargain.

    The star's loyal makeup artist, Emily Clarkson, has shared some of Michelle's go-to buys on Instagram, and you might just be surprised at how affordable they are. 

    Plenty wanted to know how the actress gets her full lip look, and Emily was happy to oblige with a snap showing off a Charlotte Tilbury lip liner and a NYX Cosmetics lipgloss.

    NYX Cosmetics Butter Gloss in Madeleine

    We love the easy-wearing product too, which gives coverage and shine without feeling sticky or heavy - no wonder laidback Michelle is a fan.

    Emily wrote on Instagram: "A sensational @michkeegan lip combo - Iconic Nude Charlotte Tilbury & @nyxcosmetics_uk Madeleine butter gloss!"

    Meanwhile, the Charlotte Tilbury 'Lip Cheat' pencil in Iconic Nude - with its neutral, beige undertone, it's perfect for adding shading to the lip and creating a fuller look.

    Charlotte Tilbury Lip Cheat in Iconic Nude

    Michelle's makeup artist Emily shared some of her favourite products

    Emily and Michelle didn't stop there with their makeup suggestions. When quizzed on their favourite foundations, Michelle confirmed hers is the Armani's Luminous Silk, while her MUA was torn between Charlotte Tilbury's Light Wonder and Dior Face & Body foundation. She also revealed that she regularly uses the IT Cosmetics CC Cream in Medium Tan on Michelle.

    The actress is also fond of The Body Shop's Vitamin E cleanser for removing her makeup.

    The Body Shop Vitamin E Cream Cleanser

    On skincare, the makeup artist added that Michelle loves to use Weleda Skin Food, as a night cream, Charlotte Tilbury's Magic Cream and Elemis face mist (like fellow star Amanda Holden).
