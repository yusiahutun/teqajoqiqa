Howard Stern, shock jock and judge of American's Got Talent, has bought a $52million beachfront mansion in Florida, it has been reported.

The home, in exclusive Palm Beach, was put on the market by textile tycoon Martin Trust and has now been sold.

The family home boasts 12.5 bathrooms and five bedrooms over 19,000 square foot on the waterfront.

The home is the biggest sale in the area this year, according to the Palm Beach Daily News.

The radio broadcaster has been reportedly searching for a home with wife Beth Ostrosky.

It appears he now plans to split his time between Florida and New York City, where he is already based.
