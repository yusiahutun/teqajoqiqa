Who Is Christin Covel?

Christin Covel is a teacher at Edison Preparatory Schools. She is charged with three charges of aggravated/indecent liberties with a minor and one count of having illicit sexual relations with a student as a teacher.

Tulsa's fugitive squad apprehended her and lodged her into the Tulsa County prison; she will be sent to Kansas to face the accusations. Moreover, she was also a match instructor in Witchita, Kansas, from 2016 to 2020, as per the public records, reported KTUL.
