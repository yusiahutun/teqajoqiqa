See Prince George Enjoy Pizza and Father-Son Time with Prince William

April 07, 2024
blog

Prince George had a fun weekend outing with his father William, Prince of Wales.

Yesterday, the royal duo made an adorable appearance at the second round of the Ashes cricket series, where England and Australia faced off at the Lord's Cricket Ground in London. The sports enthusiasts were seemingly engrossed in the game, as they were spotted making similar faces as they pointed out and discussed certain plays.

For the outing, William paired a light blue button-down shirt with a gray tweed blazer and silver-rimmed sunglasses. George opted for a checked black-and-white shirt and a dark blue sports coat with gold buttons at the cuffs.

Mike Egerton - PA Images//Getty Images

Mike Egerton - PA Images//Getty Images

During a break in the play, the pair moved to the back of their box to get refreshments, where a hungry Prince George was photographed eating a slice of pepperoni pizza as his father chatted with other guests, including British Prime Minister Rishi Sunak and Richard Thompson, chairman of the England and Wales Cricket Board.

Mike Egerton - PA Images//Getty Images

The outing comes weeks after the Wales family celebrated Father's Day, with Kensington Palace releasing a sweet photo of William with all three of his and Princess Kate's children. In the pic, the family coordinated in light blue ensembles wearing bright smiles as they pose, with George and Charlotte looking up at their father and Louis wrapping his arms over William's shoulders.

For Trooping the Colour earlier this year, the Wales kids rode with their mother Kate and grandmother Queen Camilla in the carriage procession for King Charles's birthday parade, before joining the senior working royals for a balcony appearance. The kids dressed in black and white ensembles with red accents, completing the Wales family's red-and-green color scheme, which evoked the Welsh flag.
