PNC Financial Services is one of the largest banks in the United States.

PNC Financial Services, also known as PNC Bank, is a prominent banking institution operating in the United States. With over 2,400 branches and approximately 50,000 employees, PNC has established its presence as one of the leading financial service providers in the country.

PNC Financial Services has a rich history dating back over 165 years.

Founded in 1845 as the Pittsburgh Trust and Savings Company, PNC has a long-standing history of providing financial services to individuals, businesses, and institutional clients. Over the years, the bank has grown and evolved through various mergers and acquisitions to become the respected institution it is today.

PNC is headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

The bank’s corporate headquarters are located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, where it was originally founded. From this central hub, PNC oversees its operations and strategic initiatives, focusing on delivering innovative financial solutions to its customers.
