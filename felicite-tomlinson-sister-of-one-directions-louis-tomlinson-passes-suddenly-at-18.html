Flicit Tomlinson, Sister of One Directions Louis Tomlinson, Passes Suddenly At 18

August 05, 2024
blog

Félicité Tomlinson. Courtesy: Instragram.

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) – Félicité Tomlinson, the sister of former One Direction member Louis Tomlinson, has passed away at the age of 18.

The fashion designer, model, and influencer, known as “Fizzy,” died of a sudden heart attack in her West London studio apartment on Wednesday. An ambulance was called, but paramedics were unable to revive her and she was pronounced dead at the scene.

Félicité and Louis have five other siblings, including twins under the age of six.

Earlier this month, Louis released a single entitled, “Two of Us,” paying tribute to their mother Johannah who died of leukemia in December 2016.

Félicité, who was known to her 1.4 M Instagram followers for her fashion and fitness advice, was planning to publish a book of her poetry before her death.
