Windy City Smokeout Announces 2018 Lineup

blog
The BBQ, craft beer and country music festival returns to Chicago from July 13–15, 2018, for the sixth consecutive year. According to a press release, organizers expect more than 50,000 fans on Grand Avenue near the Chicago River for the three-day event this year. Chicago barbecue joint Bub City and ACM Award-winning venue Joe’s Bar are hosting the Windy City Smokeout in 2018, and the festival once again features a wide variety of craft beers.
