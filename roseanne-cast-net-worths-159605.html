Roseanne Cast Net Worths: See How Much the Conner Family Is Worth

July 24, 2024
blog

Love them or hate them, The Conners (and company) are worth some serious cash. Question is, just how impressive are the Roseanne cast members’ net worths? From Johnny Galecki and Sara Gilbert to Roseanne Barrherself, these actors have been making audiences laugh for years — and they’ve been laughing all the way to the bank! Scroll down to see how much your favorite Roseanne cast members are worth!

What is Roseanne Barr’s net worth?

As the matriarch of the Conner family, Roseanne has to be bringing in the big bucks, right? You bet. At 65 years old, Roseanne Barr’s estimated net worth is a whopping $80 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Outside of the original and rebooted television series named after her, Roseanne has been working as an actor, comedian, author, television producer, and screenwriter, among many other roles, since the mid-80s.

What is John Goodman’s net worth?

Despite his equal acting chops, John Goodman’s net worth pales in comparison to his on-screen wife’s. According to Wealthy Gorilla, as of 2018, the 65-year-old’s net worth is an estimated $56 million. John too got his start in the mid-80s, appearing in Eddie Malcom’s Run and Face of Rage, as well as appearing in the Broadway musical Big River as Pap Finn. Eventually, he snagged his first big role in 1986 in the Coen brothers film True Stories.

What is Laurie Metcalf’s net worth?

Laurie Metcalf’s net worth hardly reflects the number of TV shows the 62-year-old actress has appeared in during her long career. According to Celebrity Net Worth, her net worth is an estimated $8 million. However, in addition to Roseanne, she has been cast for roles on Malcolm in the Middle, Dharma & Greg, The Big Bang Theory, Without a Trace, 3rd Rock from the Sun, Monk, and Desperate Housewives.

What is Sara Gilbert’s net worth?

Sara Gilbert’s role as the brooding teenager turned mother on Roseanne has certainly paid off — literally. According to Fame10, the 43-year-old has an estimated net worth of $16 million. In addition to playing the beloved role of Darlene Conner, Sara has appeared in shows like Jane the Virgin, The Big Bang Theory, Supergirl, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, and ER.

What is Lecy Goranson’s net worth?

Playing Becky Conner for five seasons on Roseanne definitely helped Lecy Goranson’s bank account. The 43-year-old actress is worth an estimated $1.5 million dollars, according to Fame10. While Lecy has been acting since the late 80s, she took a break after her Roseanne stint to focus on her education — eventually graduating from Vassar College. In addition to the family sitcom Lecy has appeared in small, but substantial Indie roles.

What is Michael Fishman’s net worth?

Even if D.J. Conner was often considered the baby of the family in the original Roseanne series, his net worth is quite impressive. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the 36-year-old is now worth an estimated $8 million. Aside from kicking it with the Conners, Michael has appeared in TV shows like Seinfeld, Hitz, and Walker, Texas Ranger.

What is Johnny Galecki’s net worth?

Last, but certainly not least, Johnny Galecki’s net worth is one of the biggest in the Roseanne bunch. According to Celebrity Net Worth, The Big Bang Theory star is worth a whopping $50 million. In addition to his mastery of sitcoms, the 43-year-old has appeared in several films including, Bean, Suicide Kings, I Know What You Did Last Summer, The Opposite of Sex, Vanilla Sky and Bookies.
