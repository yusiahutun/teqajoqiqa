Kate Upton mad at Terry Richardson over Cat Daddy bikini video

June 02, 2024
blog

She went viral in May 2012 when a video was released by controversial photographer Terry Richardson of her dancing in a tiny bikini.

And now Kate Upton has revealed that she was not exactly pleased that the clip of her performing the Cat Daddy even hit the Internet.

The 22-year-old star expressed her agitation about the now-infamous post in an interview for Vogue UK with Alexa Chung.

Without consent: Kate Upton recently revealed that she was upset over the release of her now infamous Cat Daddy dance video by controversial photographer Terry Richardson

When asked if she had confronted the 49-year-old photographer over the matter she said: 'Yes, I did. I was like, "That was disrespectful, you could have told me!"'

In the clip, a 19-year-old Upton could be seen in a teeny weeny red string bikini as she performed the dance craze by putting her 'paws' up and gyrating her hips to the 2011 hit by hip-hop group The Rej3ctz.

Chung , 31, wrote in the June issue article that the blonde beauty was 'horrified' that the video had been put on the internet as she had assumed it was being filmed for fun and wouldn't see the light of day.

Scantily clad: In the clip released in 2012, a 19-year-old Upton could be seen in a teeny weeny red bikini while performing the dance craze to the tune of hip-hop group The Rej3ctz

Skyrocketing: The video received millions of hits on YouTube and helped build her career

However, Upton could not stay upset at the dubious photographer as she said: 'Now, obviously, it's fine.'

After the video was released, the buxom beauty skyrocketed in fame resulting her getting on the coveted cover of the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue in 2013.

Alexa wrote that she asked Richardson if he feels responsible for her success to which he replied 'no' as 'Kate was born a star.'

The recent revelation only adds to the controversial reputation surrounding the famous fashion photographer as he has been accused of inappropriate conduct several times in the past few years.

British beauty: The model made the revelation to television presenter and contributing editor Alexa Chung, 31, in an article for the latest edition of Vogue UK

Controversial: Terry, 49, is the famous fashion photographer that had posted the clip

Richardson has strongly denied all accusations, which have come mostly from models he has worked with, that he acted inappropriately towards them.

Upton has since branched off her career into acting and recently revealed the exciting news that she will be starring alongside Glee star Lea Michele in William H. Macy directed comedy, The Layover.

She shared the news on Twitter two weeks ago as she shared with her 1.93million followers: 'Never thought I'd be looking forward to a layover! I can't wait!! @msleamichele @WilliamHMacy #TheLayover.'
