While she's achieved the top of the mountain in modelling, during an interview with Flaunt Magazine in 2021, Canseco desired a wish to be involved in the fashion aspect of her profession.

She said that she's since: "developed a passion for the fashion side where there’s so many incredible creative directors working alongside brilliant designers each with their own take on what fashion is, that working at that level all the time is what I’d like to aim for."

She continued: "I don’t know if my goals ever stem from a career standpoint.

"I’m a very hard worker and I have a strong work ethic, but I just really want to make sure that when I’m pushing myself to travel and work and do whatever I need to do, that I’m taking care of me and maintaining a balanced life.

"So after a year and half of forced stoppage because of COVID, I had no choice but to think about where I was, where I wanted to go, and what was really important, and I have to admit that for my mental health and focus, that down time proved to be a blessing in disguise.

"I’m just hoping now that when things get back into chaos mode that I hold onto that focus and remain happy and balanced."
