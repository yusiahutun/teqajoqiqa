Selena Gomez flies over 5000 miles to attend 'ex-boyfriend' Samuel Krost's wedding

March 05, 2024

Selena Gomez has been spotted at her ex-boyfriend, Samuel Krost’s wedding in Condecourt, France.

The former suspected celebrity couple were first linked back in 2015, when pictures of the two holding hands surfaced. Although Selena Gomez never commented on the relationship, in 2016, Sam shared a heartfelt message on his page, which ended with: “I love you Sel.”

Selena Gomez spotted at Samuel Krost’s wedding

The Single Soon singer traveled all the way to France for Sam’s wedding, who was a former talent booker for the scandalous Fyre Festival and appeared in the hit Netflix documentary.

Going for a classy look at the winter ceremony, Gomez stunned in a black fur coat over a floor-length black dress. She wore her newly blonde highlighted hair in a half up half down look, sporting effortless curls.

Samuel tied the knot with his partner, Esther Shemia, who stunned in a classic white gown with multiple layers of lace.

Gomez and Krost were last seen out in public together in 2019, in a restaurant in New York City. However, as rumors started circulating that the couple had reunited, the 31-year-old shut them down via Instagram.

Taking to her story, she penned: “I am not dating anyone. I’ve been single for two years. I’m on God’s timing not mine.”

Despite them not being romantic that year, it looks like it was a different story in 2016. In a now-deleted post, Krost wrote on social media at the time: “Selena is a real girl with a real heart and real feelings. All you superficial who are just unhappy with your love lives, understand that love is real.

“Although we have ventured on separate paths, at least I know that love exists. I love you, Sel.” 

The star is ‘casually dating’

Although she’s been rumored to be dating many celebrities, Selena hasn’t publically been in a relationship since Justin Bieber from 2017-2018.

Now, one source says she’s casually dating at the moment but doesn’t feel pressure to be in a serious relationship.

“Selena is casually dating and doing well,” the source says. “She is doing her own thing and doesn’t feel any sort of pressure to be in a relationship. She is just focusing on what’s best for her right now and has been very in tune with maintaining her own boundaries,” the insider told Entertainment Tonight.

“Her friends and family support her and just want her to be happy,” the source concluded.

The same goes with Selena towards her friends as sources also say she’s extremely happy for Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, despite previous rumors that she was concerned they were moving too fast.
