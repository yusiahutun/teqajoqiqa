Lucas Finds New Home In carbonhouse

September 30, 2024
blog

CHARLOTTE, NC (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Charlotte-based Brand consulting and design firm carbonhouse, inc. has named Brandon Lucas as Principal and Director of Marketing.


Lucas was formerly Director of Marketing for Charlotte Bobcats Arena, which opened in October 2005. In that role, he was responsible for all arena and event promotions.


Prior to working with the arena, Lucas served as Senior Vice President of Marketing for Clear Channel Entertainment’s Music Group in Indianapolis, Houston and Los Angeles.


At carbonhouse, Lucas will be responsible for marketing and joins a staff of eight that includes principals and company co-founders James Sack and Brandon Uttley.


Lucas first learned about carbonhouse when he hired the firm to redesign the website for the Charlotte Bobcats Arena in 2005.

“As a client, I was thoroughly impressed with carbonhouse,” Lucas said in a press release. “Not only do they understand technology, but they provide a high level of design in their work and take time to understand each client’s unique needs. We look forward to expanding our specialized offerings, particularly to others within the entertainment industry.”

“This is a significant addition for us,” said carbonhouse President Brandon Uttley. “Brandon Lucas is well known in marketing for the live industry nationwide and has a solid understanding of how carbonhouse can add value to venues and other businesses from a technology and branding standpoint.”
