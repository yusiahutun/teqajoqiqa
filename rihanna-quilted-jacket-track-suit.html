Rihanna Wears a Miu Miu Quilted Coat During an NYC Outing

July 28, 2024
blog

For many years, the quilted coat was associated, in my mind, with grandma fashion. In recent years, however, brands like Salvatore Ferragamo, Versace, Chloé, Max Mara, and more have heralded the high-fashion return of all things quilted, with the trend extending beyond outerwear and reimagined into skirts, dresses, and trousers.

Now, Rihanna is getting involved. And, of course, she's putting her own special spin on it.

Yesterday in New York City, the music-fashion-beauty mogul was photographed in a navy quilted bomber jacket fresh off of Miu Miu's fall/winter 2021 runway. Beneath the collared coat, she wore an oversized black track suit by Martine Rose, which featured bright-blue stripes running down the sides. She completed the outfit with Balenciaga's signature Knife boots. Accessories included white sunglasses by Loewe and leather gloves by Miu Miu.

MEGA//Getty Images

Rihanna has long sported Miu Miu when she's out and about. She wore a design from the brand this past August, while taking her niece Majesty to dinner at her favored Los Angeles restaurant, Giorgio Baldi.

Then, the "Love on the Brain" singer wore Miu Miu's crystal-embellished crepe dress in a pale-pink hue. She paired the look with strapped heeled sandals by Amina Muaddi, along with a glittery silver clutch, a crystal choker necklace, and silver earrings.

In 2019, the Fenty Beauty founder also made a statement at the airport, where she wore a Miu Miu camo cardigan, along with quilted Bottega Veneta sandals, and a Louis Vuitton cross-body bag.
