Starbucks controversial polar bear cookies left people thinking their 'throats were cut'

Starbucks customers were left horrified by a sweet treat they thought resembled a polar bear with a slit throat.

The American coffee shop giant caused controversy with a limited edition iced biscuit released for Christmas which some people completely misinterpreted.

A polar bear design sparked horror for customers who were convinced that a cute red scarf in fact looked like blood pouring from the character's neck.
