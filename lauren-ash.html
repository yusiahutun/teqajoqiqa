Career

Lauren has played several roles in Scare Tactics as well as the Canadian TV series Almost Heroes for which she won the Canadian Comedy Award for Best Female Improviser. Moreover, she also won Best Comedic Play in the year 2008 and Best Sketch Troupe in the year 2006.

This actress had a recurring role in The Ron James Show and made guest appearances in Cracked, Lost Girl, Bomb Girls, and Call Me Fitz. Her other film and TV credits include Video on Hotbox, Trial, and the Academy Award-nominated film Lars and the Real Girl. In addition, she was the voice of Sam Goldman in the animated Canadian series The Dating Guy.

Furthermore, she was part of the American sitcom called Super Fun Night alongside Rebel Wilson and Liza Lapira. Previously, this comedian appeared in the first season Comedy Central series Another Period as Hortense. However, she also won the Canadian Screen Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series at the 3rd Canadian Screen Awards for her appearance on Spun Out.

This amazing actress starred as Dina Fox from 2015 to 2021 on the NBC sitcom Superstore. Moreover, she voiced Scorpia in She-Ra and the Princesses of Power from 2018 to 2020. Then, she started a weekly podcast titled True Crime and Cocktails with her cousin Christy Oxborrow in 2020. In 2021, she also starred in the Netflix animated comedy Chicago Party Aunt.
