Celebrity Hair and Makeup Looks You Have to See

August 28, 2024

No beauty team? No problem. This week, celebrities served up stunningly simple hair and makeup looks that you can actually recreate at home without a pro team by your side. From Imogen Poots’ braids to Chanel Iman’s smokey eyes, check out the most DIY-worthy looks below.

Update your standard beachy waves with two mini French braids like actress Imogen Poots. The messier, the better.

Shout out to Taylor Schilling for showing us the power of coral lipstick. One swipe of a shiny formula and you’re totally set for the day.

Chrissy Teigen ditched her lob for a set of super realistic-looking extensions, and we’ve got to say, her loose, tousled waves have never looked better.

Chanel Iman’s glossy nude lip balanced out her smokey eye makeup. Smudge both your top and bottom liner to steal the look.

A bold eye makeup look doesn’t always come down to just eyeliner and shadow. Instead, copy Vanessa Hudgens and go load up on the mascara for long, fluttery lashes. Don’t be shy with your brow pencil, either.

The secret to wearing blush is all in the blending. Channel Holland Roden and pair your rosy flush with a crimson lipstick shade. Plus, we love a good matchy-matchy look – burgundy lips and burgundy dress? Yes, please.
