The Griffon Bleu de Gascogne is an ancient breed

The Griffon Bleu de Gascogne is a breed of dog that has a long and fascinating history. It is believed to have originated in the Gascogne region of France many centuries ago. This breed was primarily developed for hunting purposes, specifically for tracking and chasing small game such as hares and rabbits. Today, it is still highly regarded as a skilled hunting companion.

They have a unique blue ticked coat

One of the most distinctive features of the Griffon Bleu de Gascogne is its beautiful blue ticked coat. The coat is medium in length and is adorned with a pattern of small blue specks or ticks on a white background. This unique coloring sets them apart from other breeds and adds to their charm and allure.

They are known for their exceptional scenting abilities

The Griffon Bleu de Gascogne has an incredible sense of smell, which makes them highly proficient in tracking scents. They have been bred for generations to have a keen ability to follow trails and locate game.
