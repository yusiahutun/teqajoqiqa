Joel Dommett's lavish Masked Singer suits - 20k spend, secret tragedy and big donation

February 19, 2024
blog

The Masked Singer host Joel Dommett is well known for never looking less than his best when he steps out onto the stage on a Saturday Night.

Joel, 36, has amassed quite a collection of bespoke designs from his favourite tailor Joshua Kane.

In the last season of The Masked Singer, his wardrobe ended up costing in excess of £20,000 thanks to him donning a different lavish design for every episode.

However, Joel put the big spend to good use when he decided to auction off two of his favourite looks from the collection of outfits for charity.

He flogged the pricey hand-made wool and silk creations to raise funds and awareness for brain tumour charity Brain Tumour Support after his wife Hannah Cooper's mother was diagnosed with a brain tumour just before Christmas in 2020.

Joel said at the time: "We were hoping to sell at least 1,000 tickets, but smashed that target in 24 hours so the sky’s the limit.

"It would be absolutely amazing if we could raise £20,000.

"We know first-hand how much good every single pound raised will do to support individuals and families facing the shocking news of a brain tumour diagnosis.”

One snazzy metallic teal brocade creation made its way into Joel's heart as he hosted The Masked Dancer.

Sharing a few well-posed selfies in the flattering offering on Instagram he wrote: "Think this might be my fave suit ever. Really classic but with a twist."

Joel was totally proud of his orange velvet look as he bragged to his Instagram followers that it was his "fave suit ever I think" – despite his love for the previous one.

He also referenced feeling like James Bond in the design as he joked: "The names Suma. Sat Suma."

And he name-checked his favourite designer uploading another clip of himself in the outfit and posting: "The future is bright - the future is VELVET. It’s Masked Singer night babyyyyyy! @joshuakanebespoke."

Joel went for a bold red look in a Masked Singer promo shot, pulling the striking design off to perfection thanks to his chiselled good looks.

Stripping it back to something a little more simple, Joel proved it doesn't have to be eccentric if you want to stand out.

He looked equally as dashing in a classic black tie-inspired number as he does in a more fanciful offering when he attended a photo call for the ITV television show.

The suit was simple with a palette of black which featured on the tie too.

Joel saw the funny side to another design when he wore a purple silk offering for his Masked Dancer debut.

He shared a dashing photo on Instagram as he compared himself to an eggplant emoji.

He posted: "Episode 1 of Masked Dancer and I’m dressed like (eggplant emojis)..."
