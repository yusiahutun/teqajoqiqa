Actress Meagan Good has confirmed photos of her in the latest celebrity nude photo leak are real, saying the pictures were intended to be for her husband.

The Think Like a Man and Anchorman 2 star made a written statement on Instagram last night in which she said she was in 'shock' and 'saddened' at the latest leak.

Good's statement, which said those people re-posting the images should be 'ashamed' of themselves, received more than 20,000 likes.

The nude photos have emerged online after a hacker reportedly gained access to the celebrities' iCloud accounts and stole their pictures.

Apple believes the hacks are the result of 'targeted attacks' on celebrities' user names, passwords and security questions', rather than a widescale breach of their security systems. 

The FBI is investigating the hacks, although they are yet to make any arrests.
