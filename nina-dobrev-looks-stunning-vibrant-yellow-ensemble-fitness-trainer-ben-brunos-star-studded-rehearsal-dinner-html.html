Celebrity fitness trainer Ben Bruno got married over the weekend and it was a star-studded event.

Bruno shared his story of overcoming childhood trauma on the Today Show segment Overcoming the Odds.

When he was a child, his father committed suicide, which plunged him into his own depression. 

He also had back surgery for crippling pain and found his passion for fitness as a way of battling his depression and regaining strength after surgery. 

He started a blog on exercise and health and now he's the trainer to some of the biggest names in Hollywood.
