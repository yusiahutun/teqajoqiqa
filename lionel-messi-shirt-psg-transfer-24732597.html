Lionel Messi's 92 PSG shirt sells out in 'record time' after mega-money move confirmed

September 28, 2024
blog

One of the biggest transfers of all time has finally happened and Lionel Messi has a new club after 21 years at Barcelona.

Following a move to Paris Saint Germain on a free transfer, the club released the footballing icon's new shirts on their website, but have already sold out in 'record time'.

It was revealed last week that it wasn't possible for Messi to re-sign with Barca due to the club's awful financial situation, and PSG moved in to land the superstar despite the Argentine wanting to stay.

Messi now joins close friend and former teammate Neymar, in a frightening attack with Kylian Mbappe.

Lionel Messi has chosen the no.30 shirt that he wore at the start of his Barcelona career, after Ligue 1 granted PSG an exception with the number that's usually reserved for goalkeepers, with Neymar already sporting the no.10 strip that Messi has become synonymous with.

These shirts were released on PSG's online store following the announcement, and both Messi's home and away shirts sold out in under 20 minutes.

It is unknown how many were available for sale, but priced at €107.99 (£92) a piece, it was a very good 20 minutes for the French club.

The value that Messi brings to PSG is immeasurable, as they have been glorified and sedimented as perhaps the biggest club in the world following the arrival of the six-time Ballon D'or winner.

Messi will vastly increase their chances of landing Champions League glory, with the owners craving European success, and PSG will hope to go one better this season after losing in the final to Bayern Munich two-years-ago.
