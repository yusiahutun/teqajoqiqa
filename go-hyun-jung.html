Professional Career

Television works

Go Hyun-Jung made her acting debut in the 1991 South Korean television series “Eyes of Dawn.” After doing a couple of minor roles in several television series, her breakthrough role came in the year 1995.

In 1995, she played the role of Yoon Hye-rin in the SBS drama “Sandglass.” The series, which covered modern Korean history from 1970 to 1990, was one of the highest-rated dramas in Korean television history, with an average rating of 50.8% and a peak of 64.5%.
