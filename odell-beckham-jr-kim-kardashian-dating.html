Is Odell Beckham Jr Dating Kim Kardashian? Dating Rumors Reignited

June 13, 2024
blog

A star-studded Grammys party has fans asking yet again: is Odell Beckham Jr. dating Kim Kardashian? As it turns out, the rumored former flames were among the guests in attendance at one of the many pre-Grammys celebrations ahead of music’s biggest night—and their appearance at the bash is already sparking rumors of a reignited romance.

Hollywood’s finest gathered on the night of February 3, 2024, to attend events like Clive Davis’ pre-Grammys gala and rapper Jay-Z’s exclusive party. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum and her sister Khloé Kardashian both attended the latter—and so did their mutual maybe-more-than-friend, football player Odell Beckham Jr. While they arrived at the event separately, it seems that Kim and Odell ended up sharing a cozy moment together.

Related Stories

According to TMZ, rumors of a connection between Kim and Odell started swirling at the function as they were spotted chatting. Eyewitnesses claimed the two had a quiet moment together, with sources suggesting that Odell joined Kim in her range rover in the garage during the evening. However, even TMZ claims that this is all unconfirmed, so it’s best to take these rumors for what they are—just rumors.

That said, this isn’t the first time the duo has been linked. TMZ previously reported on November 8, 2023, that Kim attended Odell’s birthday party in New York after her appearance at the CFDA Awards. The event boasted a star-studded guest list, including the likes of Lil Baby, Saquon Barkley, Saweetie, Stefon Diggs, James Harden, Ashley Graham, and Justine Skye. An insider revealed to Page Six at the time that Kim and Odell spent the evening chatting with Fanatics mogul Michael Rubin. However, the source insisted that the interaction didn’t appear flirtatious.

While some sources claimed the two were merely “hanging out,” others suggested a deeper connection. Entertainment Tonight reported in September 2023 that Kim and Odell have known each other for a while and have been spending more time together, emphasizing their common circle of friends. A source close to Kim shared with the site at the time, “She’s not seriously dating anyone at the moment but is open to finding love again if she meets the right person.” The insider added, “Right now, her main focus is on her kids and her businesses.”

However, not all reports align on the romantic front. TMZ countered the dating speculation in September 2023, citing sources who claimed that Kim and Odell are just friends, with no romantic involvement between them.

Adding a twist to all of this is the fact that Odell’s romantic history includes a connection with Kim’s sister, Khloé. Back in 2016, the two were frequently photographed together, sparking dating rumors. From club appearances to birthday parties and even Drake’s Memorial Day weekend celebration, the pair’s outings fueled continued speculation. However, Khloé ended up quickly shutting down the rumors, putting those reports to rest.

Only time will tell if Kim Kardashian and Odell Beckham Jr. are the next power couple—or simply good friends enjoying their time out together. Until then, we’ll give them time to figure it all out for themselves.
