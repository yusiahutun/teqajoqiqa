Gift Experience Ideas - The Most Luxurious Gift Experiences To Give

January 29, 2024
blog

For the thrill seeker on your list, Las Ventanas al Paraiso, A Rosewood Resort in Los Cabos, has got you covered.

A bucket list one-of-a-kind gift for Formula One fan, this package offers trackside seats for the Mexican Grand Prix at the Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez circuit in Mexico City, as well as exclusive entry to the Paddock Club, the most the prestigious hospitality suite in motorsport.

In addition to serving superb food, fine wine and champagne on tap, the Paddock Club offers the opportunity to mix and mingle with some of the sport’s greatest: Lewis Hamilton, Sebastian Vettel, Kimi Räikkönen and others. Not only is this one of the final races on the Formula One calendar, it is also one of the most important. Accommodations include a three-night stay at the extraordinary 28,000 square-foot Ty Warner Mansion, considered to be one of the most luxurious villas in the world. The Ty Warner Mansion offers the same luxury amenities that one would find in a resort, such as a personal staff of seven to cater to every whim, a private beachfront infinity pool, swim up bar, palapa-covered dining patio, and a personal spa.

The unforgettable experience wraps up with private jet transfers from the race in Mexico City to Las Ventanas al Paraiso in Los Cabos. Talk about ending your trip in pure relaxation mode.

Priced at $250,000; rosewoodhotels.com.
