45 Christmas Nail Designs - Simple Christmas Nail Art Ideas

May 10, 2024
blog
1

Holiday Lights Christmas Nail Art

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Just the cutest! Start with a neutral base coat, then draw in black "cords" (we love how these have wavy loops). Apply teardrop-shaped or round nail gems in a variety of colors as "light bulbs." If you don't have nail jewels, dot nail polish on instead. Either way your nails will shine brightly all season long!

2

Holly Berry Christmas Nail Art

Bellacures

Simple holly leaves and berries decorating an accent nail or two makes for happy holiday nail art. Coordinate the berry color with the solid shade used on the other nails for a cohesive look. See more designs at Bellacures.

3

Mistletoe and Berries Christmas Nail Art

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Carry mistletoe close to you all season long—yes, on your nails! Here, a silver base coat creates a clean backdrop for mistletoe branches and dots for berries and snow. This one takes a bit of patience. We suggest trying a toothpick for drawing the greenery and a nail dotting set for the berry and snow details.

Santa Baby Christmas Nail Art

Bellacures//Getty Images

Thanks to a drip and some dots, this red-tipped manicure turns into a Santa hat masterpiece. See more designs at Bellacures.

5

White Poinsettias Christmas Nail Art

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

If you love Christmas poinsettias as much as we do, try your hand at painting them your nails. Start with a solid coat of your favorite polish, then paint on a flower shape in white. Once dry, outline in black and add other flower details as shown. Mix up the colors if you want, but we love how classy this gold and white combo looks.

Burgundy Mani Christmas Nail Art

Tatyana Maximova//Getty Images

If you want to be seasonal but not splashy, deep red nails are a solid choice for a winter manicure. This gel color is extra glossy for just a hint of glam.

Reindeer in the Snow Christmas Nail Art Idea

Evergreen Berries French Manicure Christmas Nail Art

Glitter Tips Christmas Nail Art

quiLie//Getty Images

Add glittery tips for a slightly snazzy upgrade to a rosy red manicure. Coat glitter polish in the area you want extra sparkle. Let dry in between coats and build closer to the tips as shown.

13

Holly Jolly Christmas Nail Art

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

At first glance, this Christmas holly nail art design looks complicated. But once you break it down, it's actually very simple. Start with a white or other light-colored base. Paint on green leaves in two shades, then top with three dots of red polish. Finish off the look with two sets of thin intersecting parallel lines, reminiscent of a window pane.

Pro tip: Let each layer dry fully before moving on to the next step.

Winter Blush Christmas Nail Art

Snowman Face Christmas Nail Art

Baubles and Bling Christmas Nail Art

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Stripes, glitter, and ornaments—oh my! A combination of glitter and matte polishes makes for the most creative holiday nails. We simply cannot get over the tiny bows atop the sparkly baubles!

17

Starry Night Christmas Nail Art

Bellacures

Quieter than most of the other Christmas nail art ideas you see here, this neutral take on the holidays is no less joyful. Start with a natural base, then top with white and silver stars. For visual interest, position the stars in slightly different spots on each nail. See more designs at Bellacures.

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or actually living in your own true winter wonderland, we love this design for the holiday season and beyond. Paint trees varying heights and layer as shown for a full forest effect.

Classic Colors Christmas Nail Art

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Red, green, and white are the reigning colors of Christmas. Here, each gets its due—white sparkles like snow, red berries, green holly leaves, and a peppermint-like stripe, of course.
