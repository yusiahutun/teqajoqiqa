Love Island's Elma reveals she 'loves' TOWIE co-star but is tired of getting turned down

April 20, 2024
blog

TOWIE star Elma Pazar spoke candidly about the close bond she shares with her co-star James Bennewith, better known as Diags.

The ITVBe reality show returned for its 30th season back in August and with it came newcomer Elma, who fans would recognise from the fifth series of Love Island.

The sweet relationship between the pair was evident from the first episode, with the duo joking about their plans to marry each other.

But it seems it's not all jokes as the 29-year-old admitted that it could be more than friendship from her side.

The reality star exclusively told Daily Star: "It's not friendship on my behalf. It isn't. But if he wants to keep it a friendship then that's fine.

"I love him but he doesn't love me back," she joked.

The star added: "It's gross, I'm sick of saying it. I'm not publicly releasing my love for him anymore because it's embarrassing. We plan things and they just never go through then all of a sudden it's been three months and we're both bored."

Elma shot to fame when she appeared on Love Island in 2019, entering the infamous ITV2 villa with Irish beauty Maura Higgins.

She only enjoyed a brief stint on the hit dating show, remaining for six days before being sadly dumped from the island.

The Essex beauty revealed that despite entering on day 10, she was actually meant to be amongst the original line-up which included winner Amber Gill, Tommy Fury and Anna Vakili.

The star explained: "I was meant to be in the original line-up but I actually declined it. Now looking back, if I was going to do anything different then I might as well have just done it from the beginning."

"I think it was something to with the timing being wrong. It wasn't a big deal but I had a message from the producers saying 'Are you sure that you don't want to be in the original line-up?' because you have to go into lockdown for a week before, but I was just like 'No I don't want to do it'."

She added: "But as the soon aired I was like why did I say no? But it all happens for a reason!"
