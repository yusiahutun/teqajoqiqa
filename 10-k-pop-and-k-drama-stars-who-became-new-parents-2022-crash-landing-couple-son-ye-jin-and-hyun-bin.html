Lee Jung-hyun

Actress Lee Jung-hyun from the films Decision to Leave and Peninsula gave birth to a girl in July, per Soompi. She commented on her best friend Son’s announcement post, saying that their children are also friends born in the Year of the Tiger.

The 42-year-old actress married an orthopaedic surgeon in 2019. On the TV show Omniscient Interfering View, she revealed her secret to losing 14kg after giving birth: eating a fist-sized serving of mixed-grain rice with just vegetables and protein.

Park Shin-hye and Choi Tae-joon

Another celebrity couple, Park Shin-hye and Choi Tae-joon became parents to a baby boy this May. They got married in January after four years of dating.

She also shared a pic on her Instagram of a cute family gift that she received – Nike trainers for the whole family, including the newest member!

Jeon Hye-bin

The star of popular dramas Another Miss Oh and Revolutionary Sisters, 39-year-old actress Jeon Hye-bin welcomed a baby boy in September, three years after she married a dentist in 2019, per Allkpop.

She grabbed attention for candidly sharing her pregnancy updates on social media. Now, she also shares her little daily joys with her baby, such as going for strolls to enjoy the autumn scenery and enjoying firework festivals together.

Lee Hanee

The 39-year-old actress is another famous mum of 2022. After getting married last December, Lee Hanee had her baby boy in June. As if to remind us of her Miss Korea career, she did a pregnancy photo shoot flaunting her baby bump in Vogue Korea’s June issue.

Lee has been keeping herself busy this year, returning to her profession for a Marie Claire Korea photo shoot barely two months after giving birth, and is now promoting her new film, Phantom.

Wonder Girls’ Woo Hye-rim

In February, Woo Hye-rim, who was K-pop group Wonder Girls’ lead rapper, gave birth to a baby boy after marrying taekwondo athlete Shin Min-chul in 2020 after seven years of dating.

She got a lot of love and sympathy from viewers when she openly and candidly discussed the challenges of labour and parenting on her YouTube channel Lim’s Diary.

She admitted in her video that she struggled with post-partum depression, crying every day for a week after giving birth to her first child.

TVXQ’s Changmin

Even more happy news came from the world of K-pop: TVXQ’s Changmin, who made his debut in 2004 and went solo in 2020, married a non-celebrity in the same year. He then announced the joyous news of his newborn boy through SM Entertainment in October.

He is still actively expanding his career, from taking part in SM Entertainment’s “BeYou2: The World Will Care” concert and various shows to hosting his own Naver Now radio show Max Changmin’s Freehug.

2PM’s Hwang Chan-sung

2PM’s Hwang Chan-sung was the first member of the group to become a father when his daughter was born in July. He shared his marriage and pregnancy news by posting a pic of a handwritten letter last December.

“While preparing and planning marriage with this person after my military discharge, the blessing of a new life came to us earlier than expected,” he wrote, per Soompi.

After his debut in 2PM in 2008, he is now also pursuing his career also in acting, appearing in dramas Queen for Seven Days and What’s Wrong with Secretary Kim.
