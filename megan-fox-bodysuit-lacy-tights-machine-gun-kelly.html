Megan Fox Wears a Plunging Bodysuit Tucked Underneath Lacy Tights to Support Machine Gun Kelly

February 05, 2024
blog

Megan Fox’s edgy rock-star girlfriend wardrobe has at last returned.

While supporting Machine Gun Kelly at his concert in Berlin yesterday, the Jennifer’s Body star wore an eye-catching all-black outfit. The monochromatic ensemble centered on a black bodysuit with spaghetti straps, silver buttons down the center, and a plunging scoop neckline. She appeared to tuck the bodysuit underneath sheer lacy tights, also black. Layered over the look was an oversize black leather jacket with crimson red lining, which perfectly coordinated with her bright red manicure and wavy long ginger hair. For some final touches, Fox accessorized with multiple gold chain pendant necklaces featuring an eye and a multitoned blue circle.

Frank Hoensch//Getty Images

Kelly also looked cool in his onstage outfit.

The musician wore a black fishnet long-sleeved top layered over a black T-shirt. The bottom half of his look featured more unique layers: baggy black trousers underneath half of a long blue tartan kilt. He kept the edgy theme going with his accessories, including a silver studded belt, sparkling reflective sunglasses, and chunky silver chain choker necklaces.

Frank Hoensch//Getty Images

After briefly taking a break from the public spotlight following rumors of a split, the couple have reemerged in full force.

The two officially made their red-carpet comeback last month to attend Sports Illustrated Swimsuit’s launch party in New York City. For the occasion, Fox wore a jaw-dropping black gown by LaQuan Smith, which featured a see-through mesh long-sleeved bodice with black panel cuffs on the arms and a plunging scooped neckline. It also included a shiny black sheath skirt with a subtle train.
