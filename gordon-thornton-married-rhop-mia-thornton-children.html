Mia Thorton is also a Business Woman

North Carolina native Mia Thornton is also a businesswoman like her husband. Mia, after graduation, launched her career in Charlotte, North Carolina, as a Clinic Director which eventually led to a five-year partnership with a prominent Fortune 500 company and ownership of Massage Envy Spas.

Update 2024:  Gordon Thornton and Mia's Marriage Comes to an End

In a significant turn of events, Gordon Thornton and his longtime wife, Mia Thornton, have separated. After 11 years of marriage, the couple decided to part ways in September 2023. Gordon claimed infidelity as the reason behind the separation, but they chose an amicable divorce settlement.
