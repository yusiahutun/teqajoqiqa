In the olden days, it was possible to land a great job even if you did not have a university degree. This was true for both the public and private sectors. However, in these modern times, more is required. This is why top institutions such as UDOM have become extremely popular. This university provides top-quality education, and the UDOM online application process is very straightforward for those interested in securing a place at the institution.

The University of Dodoma is located at the centre of Tanzania, 8 kilometres east of Dodoma city. UDOM is a public university that was established in March 2007 and has since made advancements to the point where it currently offers UDOM courses from seven colleges. These are the Colleges of Earth Science, Natural and Mathematical Sciences, Education, Informatics and Virtual Education, Humanities and Social Sciences (including the Institute of Development Studies, IDS), Health and Allied Science and Business Studies and Law.

UDOM online application 2020/2021

With the goal of increasing the contribution of higher education in Tanzania’s achievement of economic growth, reduction of poverty, and enhanced social wellbeing of the citizens through increased access to higher education, technological innovation, and generation and application of knowledge, UDOM University online application has been made easy for all those who are interested. The application is open from July 2020 to August 2020. First, you need to confirm if you qualify for direct entry, recognition of prior learning, or equivalent qualifications.

Eligibility for UDOM for 2020/2021

For prospective undergraduate applicants looking at UDOM online application should keep in mind the following requirements:

Direct entry

Form six applicants need a minimum of two principal passes in the required A-Level subjects with four points. This excludes three credits at O level and programs offered at the College of Health Sciences 4.5 and 5.

Equivalent qualifications

Those considering filling the UDOM application form for diploma need a minimum of Four O level passes, or NVA level III with less than four O level passes. A foreign qualification of equivalent value will also do as set forth by NECTA or VETA. Additionally, it would help if you need any of the following:

  • At least a GPA of 3.0 in an Ordinary Diploma (NTA Level 6)
  • At least a 3.0 points average for a Full Technician Certificate (FTC)
  • An average of B in Diploma in Teacher Education
  • A credit class diploma for classified diplomas and certificates
  • An average of B in health-related awards e.g., clinical medicine
  • Lower Second Class diploma for classified non-NTA diplomas

    In case you are already a graduate and wish to further your education, you need to fulfill the following requirements:

    • A filled application form
    • Referees recommendation forms
    • Academic transcripts (copies)
    • Application fee payslip, birth certificates, and other relevant documents.

    These documents are then submitted to the Director of Graduate Studies and Continuing Education of the University of Dodoma. Also, note that

    • The application fee is non-refundable and shall be indicated for your course.
    • The Tanzania Commission for Universities (TCU) will have to scrutinize any certificates from foreign institutions.
    • If you are a holder of a First or Second-class honors degree (Minimum GPA 2.7), you are safe. An average of a B in unclassified degrees from relevant disciplines and institutions are considered as well.
    • For a master’s degree, you should have a minimum GPA of 3.5.
    • PhD applicants must already possess a master’s degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 in your intended area of study.
    • If you are interested in either a master’s or a Ph.D. degree program by research only, then you need a concept paper of the discipline that you want to go for.

    UDOM online application process

    Once you are ready to proceed with the University of Dodoma online application, go to the institution's website. Create an account if you do not already have one and then go on to UDOM admission login. You will then be directed to the UDOM Online Application System. On the left pane, you will see that the portal requires the following details:

    • Personal information
    • Payment - This can be done using Airtel Money, Tigo Pesa, M-PESA, CRDB Bank, or NMB Bank. As you are applying, you will get detailed information on how to pay using either of these mediums.
    • Academic results
    • Education qualifications
    • Attachment panel
    • Employment record
    • Financial arrangements
    • Referee details
    • Choosing the program you want

    Once you have filled all these in and made the payments, you are ready to review your application and submit it. The system works with both Tanzanian and international people who want to apply. The good thing is that you do not need to complete the entire application process in one sitting.
