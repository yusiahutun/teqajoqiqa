&quot;Nun Not Pregnant, It's Medical Condition&quot;: Fact Checkers Clear Air on Trending Photo

August 04, 2024
blog
  • A photo of a nun doing rounds on social media has elicited mixed reactions as netizens questioned why the catholic sister was pregnant
  • Fact Checkers from Africa Check followed up on the picture to ascertain its eligibility and found out that the nun was not pregnant
  • The picture was from a Diocese in Yaounde Cameroon, the church clarified that the nun was not pregnant but had a medical condition

A photo doing rounds on social media of a woman in a white habit of the type worn by nuns in the Catholic Church has elicited mixed reactions.

A section of social media users made fun of the picture saying they had never seen a pregnant nun while others questioned the Catholic faith.

“I thought I had seen it all then boom a pregnant sister(nun),” a Facebook user commented on the picture.

According to Africa Check, Catholic nuns take vows of poverty, chastity and obedience.

The vows require them to give up the right to personal property, to marry or to engage in coitus. The Priests take the same vows. Nuns and priests are therefore not supposed to have children.

Picture is from Cameroon

The fact-checkers said the woman in the picture is from an Archdiocese in Yaoundé Cameroon.

The archdiocese said the woman was not pregnant but had an underlying condition.

"When falsification invades the world, there is no place left for satire and infected speech...To all of us who are shocked by the indecent posts about the sister, may the Lord give us the strength to stand up and heal the sister," Archidiocèse de Yaoundé said in a Facebook post.

Netizens react

On Facebook, Isaac Kim differed with the opinion of others, saying it was possible for Catholic Nuns to be pregnant.

"Lie, am not a catholic though it's possible for any NUN to get pregnant but she will ever be in her regalia in public not at all, this might be a movie scene or something is wrong with her health," Kim said.

Goodness Jonah asked how she would be pregnant and still serve in the Catholic faith.

"How would she be pregnant and still be walking on the street with the revered HABIT (nun's dress) Catholics don't do like that ooo... This is certainly a health challenge! May God touch her!" Jonah said.

Yifeanyi Nzoputa was not puzzled by the picture, saying it was not a new thing.

"It's not a new thing, maybe they decide to come clean somehow, the thing wey dey hungry pregnant woman dey hungry the man wey give am bele," Yifeanyi said.

Catholic Church investigates how two nuns became pregnant

In November 2019, the Catholic Church in Rome launched an investigation into strange circumstances under which two nuns who took the vow to keep chastity rules got pregnant while on a mission in Africa.

The African nuns who belong to different orders in Sicily in Italy were posted to their home country in Madagascar to carry out missionary services but slipped up and got pregnant.

One of the women, 34, discovered she was pregnant when she underwent a medical scan after complaining of stomach pains, The Sun reported.

Her counterpart whose age was not disclosed but is said to be a mother superior (head of a female religious community) left her mother country after realising she was one month pregnant.
