Lorraine viewers blast Ranvir for talking over Mick Hucknall in 'awkward' comeback interview

Lorraine viewers have criticised stand-in presenter Ranvir Singh for constantly talking over Mick Hucknall during his exclusive comeback chat.

The Simply Red star was promoting his new album and tour and also mentioned that he is on "voice rest" - just as well, fans said, as he couldn't get a word in edgeways.

Mick, 62, had also "aged well" Twitter users said, but the chat was "awful" with Ranvir's interview style really jarring with fans.
