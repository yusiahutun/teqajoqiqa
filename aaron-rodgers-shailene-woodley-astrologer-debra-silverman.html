Did Aaron Rodgers and Shailene Woodley's Astrologer Introduce Them?

June 04, 2024
blog

Aaron Rodgers and Shailene Woodley's quickie engagement may have surprised fans, but it could have been a case of cosmic intervention. According to Page Six, both Rodgers and Woodley see celebrity astrologer Debra Silverman and their relationship could have gotten a little help from the stars.

Silverman boasts an A-list group of followers, which includes Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, and Cara Delevingne, in addition to Woodley and Rodgers. Silverman mentioned meeting Woodley on her website and their shared love for astrology and metaphysics. Woodley, who was born on November 15, 1991, is a Scorpio. Rodgers, born on December 2, 1983, is a Sagittarius.

"I had the pleasure of sitting down recently with this young woman to discuss her interest in astrology, our mutual ability to delve into metaphysics, and our love for truth," Silverman wrote of meeting Woodley on her website.

In 2014, Woodley told Cosmopolitan that she leans on astrology when she's looking for love, saying, "I'm so that chick. I'm like, 'Hey, what's yo name? What's yo sign?'"

As for Rodgers, he attended Silverman's Conversations with Destiny summit in 2020.

"Did you know nothing [about astrology] when I met you?" Silverman asked him during their sit-down.

"My only connection to it was I love doing the crossword in the daily paper and I would read my Sagittarius horoscope and that was it," he said, though things have clearly changed.

He went on to say that after he was introduced to Silverman by his ex, Danica Patrick, he started to get more and more interested in astrology.

"At your concert, there was a woman named Debra Silverman ... and Danica interviewed her for the podcast. I got to meet her, I got to get a reading, and it just spiked my interest," Rodgers said during an Instagram Live session with Patrick and musician Nahko. "It's just fascinating how accurate it can be when you start to understand your natal chart and it's something that's really kept my interest and it's fascinating to me and I'm excited every time I learn more about it."

Neither Rodgers nor Woodley have commented about how they met and whether or not Silverman was involved in the process. However, astrologer Maressa Brown says that in addition to sharing an astrologer, the newly engaged couple share another cosmic connection: Venus in Libra, making their love languages very, very similar.

"Woodley and Rodgers were both born with their Venus (the planet of romance) in Libra, indicating that they basically have the same love language," she said. "What's more, Rodgers's moon, a.k.a. his emotional compass, is in Scorpio. Not only was Woodley born with her sun in Scorpio, but when she was born, sexy Mars and powerful Pluto were there, too — and all in the fifth house of romance! — so that's even further indication that they're in tune emotionally and are destined to derive power from their pairing."
