While modeling remains her most recognized venture, Hailey Baldwin has dabbled into other career facets, including a handful of movies and TV shows.

Hailey Baldwin Made Headlines in March 2022 following reports of a health scare involving a blood clot. She got discharged from the hospital hours later and has since made several public appearances, proving she was out of danger.

The model graced her husband Justin Bieber’s concert in Utah, showing support for his music throughout the show. In a most-enviable moment, the singer dedicated his song, “Anyone” to his beautiful wife just before performing it on stage.

He accompanied it with some beautiful words of praise, ultimately making her the center of attention at the show.

While constantly being in Justin’s spotlight through moments like this has undoubtedly rubbed off on her, Hailey has also made a name for herself in her own rights.

She has roots in dancing and modeling but has made more on-screen appearances in the last decade. Here we discuss some movies and TV shows Hailey Baldwin has starred in throughout her showbiz career.

How Many Movies And TV Shows Has Hailey Baldwin Starred In?

Hailey Baldwin has appeared in several movies and TV shows including “Saturday Night Life,” “Livin It: Unusual Suspect,” and “Drop The Mic.”

However, most of these appearances have been either as herself or a guest star. She has also featured in several music videos, and documentaries and runs the web series “Who’s In My Bathroom.”

Hailey Baldwin’s Movies And Television Appearances

Born to Stephen Baldwin of the Baldwin Brothers, and his wife Kennya Deodato, Hailey Baldwin got her start in showbiz at a young age.

At nine years old, the star appeared alongside her famous family on the show, “Livin It: Unusual Suspect.” Years later, the then-teenager featured in an episode of “Saturday Night Live” beside her famous uncle Alec Baldwin.

She went on to appear in the documentary movie, “Secrets Of New York Fashion Week” in 2015 and the 2021 TV series, “Dave.” 

In 2021, Hailey starred alongside Justin Bieber in his documentary movie, “Justin Bieber: Our World.’

Other notable on-screen appearances include cameos in “Ocean’s 8,” and “Love Advent.” Additionally, she has credits in 2021’s “Jungle Red,” “Fyre,” 2018, and “Fyre Fraud,” 2019.

Other On-Screen Ventures

Hailey Bieber snagged a spot as a co-host at the 2018 iHeartRadio Music Awards. But before that, she already established a career in show-hosting, albeit on television.

One of her earliest gigs was as a TV host in a segment of the 2015 MTV Europe Music Awards. A year later, she co-hosted the 2016 iHeart Music Video Awards.

She went on to snag a hosting spot on the TBS show, “Drop The Mic,” alongside Method Man. She has also guest appeared on “Live With Kelly And Ryan,” “The Late Late Show With James Corden,” and “Watch What Happens Live” among others.

Aside from her appearances in movies and TV shows, Hailey Baldwin has appeared as herself in various music videos. She played Cody Simpson’s love interest in the music video of the song, “On My Mind.”

The model also appeared in the music video for “Love To Love You Baby,” and has been featured in several of Justin’s works. She has a YouTube channel where she hosts the show, “Who’s In My Bathroom,” featuring guest appearances from various celebrities.

What Else Is Hailey Baldwin Known For?

Previously a Ballet dancer, Hailey Baldwin gave up her dancing dreams to begin a modeling career following a leg injury. Foraying into modeling eventually paid off, shooting the 25-year-old to fame under IMG Models.

As a model, she has appeared on the cover of top magazines like “Vogue,“ “L’Officiel,” and “Harper’s Bazaar” among others.

She has also served as the face of several product brands and campaigns, including the “Power Of Good” campaign for bareMinerals make-up brand.

Hailey Baldwin’s Entrepreneurial Pursuits

Notably, Hailey’s work in the entertainment industry over the years has helped her establish her own entrepreneurial ventures. In 2016, Daily Edited collaborated with her to release a capsule collection of leather fashion goods dubbed TheHaileyedited.

That same year, the television personality launched her line of footwear in collaboration with the British brand, Public Desires. She also debuted her makeup line, teaming up with Aussie beauty company, ModelCo.

These numerous ventures have earned the model, dubbed the industry’s “fresh face,” a net worth of $20 million.

Despite her numerous achievements, Hailey Baldwin’s marriage to Justin Bieber is perhaps what she is best known for. Since tying the knot, the two young lovers have worked together on occasions.

They have also stirred media frenzies with their envy-inducing PDAs and steamy photos for joint magazine covers, making their relationship subject to public speculations.

But these two seem not to care, striving only to focus on each other and their enduring love.
